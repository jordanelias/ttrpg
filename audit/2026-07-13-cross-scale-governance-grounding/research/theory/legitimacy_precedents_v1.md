## 1 · Mandate of Heaven (Han China / *tianming*) — the archetype [REAL]

**The single most load-bearing finding: it answers BOTH halves of the headline gap** (L/PS inert
*and* Mandate-has-no-collapse-mechanism), and it is *not* the same as Valoria's existing "Mandate" stat.

- **Conditional & revocable from the founding text.** *Shangshu* has the Zhou justify regicide by Heaven
  *withdrawing* the mandate for misrule — and writes the **famine-as-withdrawal-signal in from the start**
  ("by your dissoluteness… heaven has cast us off, so that there is distress for want of food"). Mandate
  is a **performance lease, not a bloodline right** — which is what licenses rebellion.
- **The collision is a CONJUNCTION of two independent signals, not a cascade** (Dong Zhongshu's *zaiyi*
  science): (a) an **elite/court channel** — astronomical portents, omens, publicly displayed; (b) an
  independent **popular channel** — real regional suffering read from below as indictment. A bad harvest
  *alone* is a settlement Order/Dearth problem; a portent *alone* is a court ritual. **The legitimacy
  crisis is the resonance of both arriving at the top tier at once** — and a portent can fire with **no
  geographic locus at all** (celestial), i.e. the top-down arm arrives *independently* of the bottom-up arm.
- **Two mechanically-distinct relief valves** (the transferable part): **(1) scapegoat-the-subordinate**
  — a designated minister/governor absorbs the cosmic-political liability (Chancellor Zhai Fangjin's 7 BCE
  suicide after a Mars/Antares portent; emperors forced chancellors to resign after celestial events, the
  emperor's person being un-blamable by design) → a **bend-around** that routes the hit off the top
  authority onto an appointee, at the cost of *their* standing/office/life; **(2) ruler self-reproach** —
  a costly-signal apology spending the *ruler's own* resource (Emperor Wen's agriculture edict; Wu's
  "Repenting Edict of Luntai"; Qianlong's drought self-reproach rituals — retreat, reduced meals, era-name
  change).
- **Contestable, not automatic** (Xunzi's *Tian Lun*: Heaven is "constant and amoral," portents are not
  real communication). The disaster→verdict chain is a **rhetorically contestable interpretive move**,
  open to a naturalist counter — a natural fit for a **court social-contest scene**, and a lever a rival
  can wield (*declaring* a disaster a legitimacy-sign is itself a political act).
- **Withdrawal's real output is a cross-scale TRANSFER of power, not a stat flip.** The Han fell not to the
  Yellow Turban rebellion (184 CE) directly but because suppressing it forced the center to devolve lasting
  military authority to regional governors, who never returned it → warlordism → Three Kingdoms. **Collapse
  = national→province/territory power devolution.**
- **Vertical spine:** Emperor → Inspector/Governor → commandery Administrator → county Magistrate (the
  "father-mother official," responsible for granary/famine-relief), with promotion/demotion flowing back up
  — a bidirectional cascade. **Remonstrance** (the Censorate) = a *duty*-shaped role obligated to make the
  Mandate-relevant criticism. **Legalism-under-Confucianism** = a form(ideology)-vs-function(admin-logic)
  split — exactly `governance_type_registry`'s FLAG-doesn't-guarantee-the-VECTOR-beneath pattern.

**Valoria hooks:** grounds **GAP-B2** (Mandate withdrawal), **GAP-E1/E2** (the two-signal collision
primitive), **GAP-D-adjacent** (scapegoat bend-around). **"False-friend" flag:** Valoria's `Mandate =
round(7T/(T+6))` shares the *name* only — no interpretive/event/withdrawal layer. Supports/sharpens **D4**
(whichever mechanic carries collapse needs an interpretive/portent layer, not just a lower floor-avg).
**Verdict: REAL**, unusually strongly attested (founding-text→Qing, named institutional carriers).

## 2 · Bodin — indivisible sovereignty [REAL — sharpest actionable finding]

- Sovereignty is **absolute, perpetual, indivisible**; splitting the law-giving power between ≥2 agents is
  a **category error** producing anarchy "worse than the cruelest tyranny" (written amid the French Wars of
  Religion's competing supreme-authority claims). Indivisible ≠ unlimited (still bound by natural/divine/
  fundamental law). A **corporate body** can hold it, provided its decision is treated as one undivided act.
- **Diagnostic:** sovereignty sits with **whoever can bindingly declare war/peace without anyone's
  sign-off** — Bodin applied it to unmask Venice's "mixed constitution" as really an aristocracy.

**Valoria hook — grounds GAP-D1 directly:** `scale_hierarchy §5.3` gives Monarch **and** Parliament each
an unconditional chain-bypass with **no precedence** — *exactly* the two-unlimited-sovereigns configuration
Bodin's theory exists to forbid. Two real resolutions: **(1)** nest one inside the other (Tokugawa/English
constitutional-monarchy — one closure point even though two bodies act); **(2)** make Monarch-vs-Parliament
collision a **named designed crisis mode** (the English-Civil-War case). His war/peace test = a mechanical
check for where sovereignty really sits in any ambiguous multi-body governance-mode. **Verdict: REAL.**

## 3 · Tokugawa — auctoritas/potestas split [REAL]

- A genuine institutionally-enforced split: shogun = **potestas** (force, *kokudaka* land, foreign policy);
  emperor = **auctoritas** (ritual/legitimacy, era-names, court rank). Enforced by the **Kinchū narabi ni
  kuge shohatto (1615)** (confined the emperor to "scholarship and poetry"; court appointments needed
  shogunal approval) + the standing Kyoto Shoshidai overseer. **Yet every shogun's title was formally
  conferred by the emperor** — the apex always *needed* him.
- **The transferable mechanism:** borrow legitimacy **once, at the apex**, then run an independent potestas
  chain beneath (shogun→daimyo via *kokudaka*/sankin-kōtai) that **never re-touches the source** — propped
  by its own instruments (hostage-kin, fiscal exhaustion, rotation). Safe *only because* asymmetric &
  enforced (one coercive, one ratification-only-with-a-leash).
- ***murauke*** (village-collective tax liability, headman-collected, affluent villagers covering
  shortfall; magistrates "rarely showed up except for annual inspections") = a **bottom-up
  settlement-internal fiscal self-aggregation** primitive — the settlement governs its own internal
  distribution of an externally-imposed obligation, dropping the center's enforcement cost to near-zero.

**Valoria hooks:** the asymmetric-enforced split is the *safe* model for **GAP-D1** (Monarch/Parliament) —
and a warning that two *symmetric* unlimited bypasses is the *unsafe* config. **murauke** = a bottom-up
primitive that keeps a collision from cascading past the settlement (feeds **GAP-E3** dampening). **Verdict:
REAL** (co-signed instrument, dated). Distinct from the roster's F7 "hold the Son of Heaven" custody-seizure
mechanic — do not conflate.

## 4 · Machiavelli — *grandi/popolo*, feared/loved [REAL]

- ***grandi* vs *popolo*** (the two humors): the great wish to command/oppress; the people wish only not to
  be oppressed. **Which bloc elevated a ruler has lasting structural consequence** — a noble-elevated prince
  is surrounded by self-regarding peers (coup-exposed); a people-elevated prince stands alone over a
  populace expecting only non-oppression (commands absolutely but on a volatile charismatic base).
- **Feared vs loved = a controllability asymmetry:** fear is prince-controllable (institutional); love is
  fortuitous, populace-driven, "treacherously shifty." Avoid *hatred* (seizing property) — hatred, not low
  popularity, topples princes. *virtù/fortuna* = pre-built adaptive capacity against shocks (a contrast lens
  to *tianming*'s moralized cosmos — two temperaments reacting to the *same* famine).

**Valoria hooks:** *grandi/popolo* gives the governance-**mode** FLAG a **failure-mode signature**
(elite-satisfying → lateral peer-coup risk; populace-satisfying → raw PS volatility) — feeds **GAP-C2/C3**
and the intrapopulation-conflict thread. Feared/loved = a **mechanical constraint** on which stat a verb
may reliably move (L-levers ruler-actionable; PS-levers exogenous/stochastic) — feeds the key registry.
**Verdict: REAL** (canonical primary-text). *(Corpus note: the existing repo mines the* Discorsi*'s
republicanism — a different argument — so the* Prince *material here is genuinely new.)*

## 5 · Venice — the "myth of Venice" [REAL, distinct from the mined procedural machinery]

The lot/election/term-limit/collective-magistracy machinery is **already mined** (2026-06-28 & 07-09

Corroborated by Bodin's contemporary skepticism (the mixed-constitution image doesn't survive his test).

**Valoria hook:** a **narrative-control / suppression lever** distinct from the Compact/Ledger machinery —

consequence-bearing, and its cross-scale concealment). **Verdict: REAL** (bend-around/outward direction).

## 6 · Classical (Greek/Roman) — [REAL; full synthesis pending-append]

Confirmed load-bearing items (adversarially spot-verified against primary texts):
- **Anacyclosis** (Polybius) — the constitutional growth-and-decay cycle + the Roman mixed constitution
  (consuls/senate/tribunes as mutually-checking balanced authorities). Directly grounds a general **regime
  DECAY** function. *Caveat:* von Fritz/Moatti note Polybius idealizes and marginalizes the tribunate (the

  Valoria's **L vs Mandate/Military**. Modern scholarship treats the clean trichotomy as retrospective
  systematization — cite as a *distinction*, not a rigid Roman working vocabulary.
- **Cicero, *De Re Publica* 1.39** (verified: "*res publica res populi…*") — legitimacy grounded in the
  people, the consent basis of L.
- ***cursus honorum*** = a real **Standing ladder** with age/office gates; **tribune veto** = a
  **bend-around** authority.

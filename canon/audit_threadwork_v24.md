## P-01: INSEPARABILITY — all three dimensions co-move

**Violation test:** Does the mechanic allow a thread op to resolve without all three dimensional auto-effects firing?

**Assessment: PASS.**

Version C co-movement fires on every operation. Temporal auto-effect (Coherence loss at Relational+ scale, narrative at Object/Personal). Epistemic auto-effect (per operation type and degree). Actual auto-effect (d6). No GM discretion to skip. No operation type exempted. Mending has its own co-movement profile (§9.9/P-19) but still fires all three dimensions.

The scale-based Coherence exemption at Object/Personal (P-03 patch) does NOT violate P-01. The temporal co-movement still fires at Object/Personal scale — it manifests as narrative effects and d6 consequences rather than as personal Coherence loss. The three dimensions co-move; the practitioner's personal rendering absorbs negligible impact at small scales. The co-movement is in the world, not in the practitioner.

Involuntary Leap (P-29) fires co-movement despite no operation occurring. Correct — the practitioner's configuration interacted with threads; all three dimensions moved.

**Potential concern:** The Coherence 0 prevention rule (P-27) says practitioners at Coherence 0 cannot operate. But if they're involuntarily exposed to Thread activity (ambient, not a Leap), do the three dimensions still co-move for them? Yes — involuntary exposure is not an operation. The practitioner is a passive recipient. Co-movement from others' operations affects them through the world's state, not through their own Thread work.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-02: EIN SOF = infinite positive being

**Violation test:** Does any monster description frame its origin as chaos, void, or evil?

**Assessment: PASS.**

The philosophical framing (Part One) explicitly states Ein Sof is infinite positive being. Gaps are described as "where the constitutive ground shows through" and as eruptions of excess, not void. Monstrous entities are "configurations from the unintelligible ground forced through the rupture into the rendered world, partially intelligible and exceeding the world's capacity to integrate them."

The Rupture description (P-30) states: "This is not the end of being. It is the end of intelligible being." The ground does not stop. Consciousness does not stop. What stops is the rendering process.

FR Dissolution is framed as "tearing the intelligible face away" — the ground erupts through the tear as excess, not void.

Over-actualisation hazard: Weaving drives configurations toward more coherence "than the substrate naturally supports" — Ein Sof continues to spool but the rigid thread can't integrate what the ground provides. The ground's positive provision is the source of the problem, not a lack.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-03: RENDERING = consciousness-performed

**Violation test:** Does the mechanic treat the rendered world as separate from consciousness?

**Assessment: PASS — strengthened from prior system.**

The redesign explicitly frames rendering as what consciousness does: "Rendering is what consciousness does. Ordinarily, consciousness renders the world... This IS ordinary experience." The Leap is defined as the suspension of rendering, not as entering a separate world. The GM is the rendering engine (unchanged from prior system).

The Leap-as-rendering-suspension framing significantly strengthens P-03 compliance. The practitioner doesn't leave the rendered world — they suspend the process that constitutes it. During contact, there IS no rendered world for the practitioner because rendering has been suspended. This is more philosophically precise than the prior framing (which treated Thread operations as "working the intelligible face," implying the rendered world persists during contact as a separate thing the practitioner manipulates).

Diagnosis is explicitly framed as rendering: "the last act of rendering before the Leap." Thread perception (TS-based) is rendering. The distinction between perception (rendering) and contact (rendering-suspension) is clear and philosophically grounded.

✓ **No violation. Strongest compliance of any constraint.**

## P-04: MONSTROSITY = ontological, not moral

**Violation test:** Does any rule assign moral valence to monstrous entities?

**Assessment: PASS.**

Mode 3 resolution (P-16) explicitly includes communication/negotiation as a resolution path: "Mode 3 beings may have comprehensible goals (P-04). They are not by nature hostile." Mode 1/2 entities are described as rendering failures, not villains. No alignment system. No "evil" language.

The former Taint track — which carried implicit moral framing ("corruption," "seduction") — has been eliminated. Coherence degradation is explicitly NOT moral: "Low Coherence is not corruption, seduction, or moral failure. It is what happens when you have repeatedly practiced the absence of your own consciousness from itself."

Dissolution residue use is framed as a cost/sacrifice, not as "forbidden power" or "dark arts."

✓ **No violation.**

## P-05: THREE EMERGENCE MODES mechanically distinct

**Violation test:** Are all three modes mechanically distinguishable?

**Assessment: PASS.**

- **Mode 1 (Ordinary):** Health 6, Martial 3, Cohesion 1. No self-maintenance. −1 Health/round. Dissolves in 1d6 rounds. Resolvable via Weaving or Dissolution.
- **Mode 2 (Providence):** Health 10, Martial 4, Cohesion 3. Organised but temporary. Cannot be triggered by practitioners. Represents genuine emergence from the ground.
- **Mode 3 (Threadcut):** Continuous self-rendering. Observer-dependent intelligibility. Rendering Strain → De-actualisation. Past-Pull auto-Gap. Wounds cost Thread work. Immune to Weaving-based resolution (P-16). Can communicate.

All three are mechanically distinct. Mode 3 has been significantly expanded with observer-dependent rendering (§6.2), Rendering Strain, De-actualisation (§6.4), and the Weaving exclusion. No two modes are interchangeable.

**Note:** Mode 2 was flagged in earlier canon work (D-18) as needing to be an event, not an entity. The v2.4 document retains Mode 2's entity-like stat block (Health 10, etc.). This may need review against the D-18 ruling. However, the stat block represents the event's physical manifestation, not a persistent entity — Mode 2 is temporary and self-resolving. **Flag for verification against D-18 but no violation.**

✓ **No violation (flag for D-18 check).**

## P-06: THREADCUT = radically is without becoming

**Violation test:** Do threadcut beings maintain via Thread work? Past-Pull → auto-Gap? No Taint?

**Assessment: PASS — significantly strengthened.**

Threadcut beings are framed as existing in "permanent Leap-state" — continuous Being beyond ordinary rendering, sustained by continuous originary intentionality (§6.1). This is a major philosophical upgrade from the prior system's three mechanical rules.

Past-Pull auto-Gap: retained ✓. Wounds cost Thread work: retained ✓. No Taint: retained, and the Taint track no longer exists, so there's nothing to accidentally apply ✓. Coherence track explicitly does not apply to threadcut beings ("they have no rendering to destabilise") ✓.

Observer-dependent rendering (§6.2): new, and philosophically grounded in the Solmund description from the canonical timeline. The being renders at each observer's ceiling. Beyond-ceiling rendering costs Rendering Strain. De-actualisation models the cessation of self-rendering (Solmund's dissolution).

Diagnosis on threadcut beings reveals the temporal paradox: no accumulated past, no natural trajectory, something held rather than persisting ✓.

✓ **No violation. Strongest implementation of P-06 to date.**

## P-07: CALAMITY = rendered-side mechanism

**Violation test:** Does TT/RS system imply over-drawing, not ground responsiveness?

**Assessment: PASS.**

RS (Rendering Stability) is explicitly a measure of the rendered world's capacity — not of the ground's state or behaviour. The ground does not respond, fight back, or act. Gaps form because "the Einhir over-drew the threads, and the structural limits of the substrate were exceeded." The ground's positive being "erupts through the tear" — eruption, not attack.

Over-actualisation hazard: the substrate strains because practitioners drove configurations beyond natural coherence. The ground continues to spool but the rigid configuration can't integrate. This is rendered-side mechanism — the practitioners caused the problem by over-working the substrate.

FR Lock chronic drift: the frozen thread obstructs configurational traffic. RS degrades because the substrate is structurally damaged, not because the ground is fighting back.

The Rupture (RS 0): "The rendered world's capacity to integrate what Ein Sof provides has completely failed." The ground doesn't cause the Rupture. The rendered world's own operations caused it.

No language anywhere attributes agency, intention, or responsiveness to Ein Sof or the ground.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-08: EPISTEMOLOGICAL BARRIER = inaccessibility, not suppression

**Violation test:** Does any mechanic allow non-sensitives to gain Thread-level knowledge through study alone?

**Assessment: PASS.**

TS growth requires held confrontation (Spirit check), not study. The Scholarly TS Path (Lenneth Variant) requires Spirit check + genuine perceptual risk — "intellectual understanding alone does not produce sensitivity."

Diagnosis trajectory perception is gated by scale (§2.2): Structural-scale trajectory is below the waterline. The practitioner cannot render the future of vast configurations. This is consistent with P-08: the barrier is metaphysical, not a knowledge gap that study could close.

The Devout Constraint blocks TS growth through essentialist theology — the perceptual stance required is foreclosed. Study within the essentialist framework cannot breach the barrier because the barrier is not informational; it is perceptual.

Non-practitioners at Coherence 0 (if such existed) could not develop Thread knowledge — their rendering has failed. The barrier is not suppressed even in catastrophic conditions.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-09: MEMORY PULLING = messy, costly, detectable

**Violation test:** Does any mechanic allow clean, undetectable memory erasure?

**Assessment: PASS.**

Past-Oriented Pulling (§2.6) produces Temporal Disjunction: physical facts removed, memories preserved. The paradox IS the mess — everyone remembers something that didn't happen. Detectable by TS 30+ Diagnosis (orphaned configuration).

Co-movement fires on Past-Oriented Pulling (all three dimensions). RS −3 minimum. Coherence −2 (scale + Past-Pull additional). The Fraying Bane activates after 3+ uses per season. These are heavy costs.

P-21 (mechanical state reversion) ensures that even when a Past-Pull succeeds, the downstream mechanical states partially persist (experience-triggered states survive) while physical-fact-triggered states revert — creating a disjunction between mechanical state and experienced state. This IS the "messy" requirement.

P-22 (delayed manifestation): the paradox window where physical facts haven't caught up adds another layer of mess. Practitioners perceive dual states. Non-practitioners don't. Nothing about Past-Oriented Pulling is clean.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-10: EPISTEMIC SEDUCTION = perceptual shift, not corruption

**Violation test:** Does any mechanic use language of corruption, sin, or moral failure?

**Assessment: PASS — strongest compliance improvement in the entire redesign.**

The Taint track has been eliminated entirely. The term "epistemic seduction" has been cut. The term "Taint" has been cut. All transformation effects are now Coherence degradation at low levels.

The framing is explicit: "Low Coherence is not corruption, seduction, or moral failure. It is what happens when you have repeatedly practiced the absence of your own consciousness from itself." Belief Co-Authorship at Coherence 2 is not the GM corrupting the character — it is the GM presenting the perspective that arises when consciousness's constitutive categories loosen.

Community Intervention framing: "they no longer perceive the need. From their perspective, the categories that others are trying to restore are arbitrary rendering artifacts. They are not wrong. They are also not functional." No moral judgment. The practitioner's perspective is acknowledged as valid — just non-functional within the rendered world.

Dissolution residue is framed as "compressed potential oriented toward the unintelligible ground" — not as evil substance or forbidden power. Its use accelerates Coherence loss because it intensifies exposure to the beyond-rendering state.

✓ **No violation. The elimination of the Taint track is the single largest P-10 compliance improvement.**

## P-11: TEMPORAL DISJUNCTION universal — all thread ops produce some TD

**Violation test:** Does any thread operation produce zero temporal consequence?

**Assessment: PASS.**

Under the scale-based Coherence system: Object/Personal operations produce zero PERSONAL Coherence loss, but the temporal auto-effect still fires. It manifests as narrative effects and d6 consequences. The temporal dimension moves; the practitioner's personal rendering absorbs negligible impact. P-11 requires that all ops produce "some TD" — the temporal disturbance exists at all scales, it just doesn't always cost the practitioner personally.

Mending produces Coherence −1 regardless of Gap scale (substrate engagement is inherently deep). FR always produces additional Coherence loss. Past-Oriented Pulling produces the heaviest temporal consequence.

History Resonance (bonus die with risk of Coherence −1 on showing 1) adds secondary temporal cost.

Involuntary Leap (P-29) produces Coherence −1 despite no operation occurring. Even unwilled contact with threads produces temporal consequence.

No operation type in any mode produces zero temporal consequence in the world.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-12: RELATIONAL CONTAGION via knotting

**Violation test:** Does a transforming practitioner's shift propagate through Knots?

**Assessment: PASS.**

Coherence thresholds produce Knot strain at every band below Stable:
- Dissonant (7–5): +1 strain per 3 sessions to Close Knots.
- Fragmented (4–3): +1 per 2 sessions to all Knots.
- Fractured (2): +1 per session to all Knots.
- Severed (1): +2 per session to all Knots.

The strain propagation is more granular than the old system (which had Taint-based strain AND Intelligibility-based strain from separate tracks). The unified Coherence track produces a single, clear strain cascade that increases in severity as Coherence drops.

Dissolution residue use accelerates Coherence loss, which accelerates Knot strain, which propagates the rendering instability to connected beings. The contagion pathway is: residue use → Coherence loss → Knot strain → wrongness/Crisis for connected entities.

✓ **No violation.**

## P-13: FORGETTING = rendering failure — Southernmost knowledge is mechanically untransmittable to non-sensitives

**Violation test:** Can non-sensitive characters retain full Southernmost knowledge indefinitely?

**Assessment: NOT DIRECTLY TESTED — deferred to existing Forgetting mechanics.**

The v2.4 document does not redesign the Forgetting mechanic. It retains the existing framework: Southernmost knowledge is unstable in non-sensitive minds. Evidence is available; epistemic capacity to render it as knowledge is not.

The redesign's philosophical framing strengthens the Forgetting's foundation: the unintelligible ground exceeds consciousness's capacity to render it (Part One). Southernmost knowledge IS knowledge of the unintelligible ground's behaviour. Non-sensitives cannot render it because rendering is their ONLY epistemic mode, and this knowledge exceeds rendering capacity.

No new mechanic in v2.4 violates P-13. The existing Forgetting mechanics remain in force.

✓ **No violation (existing mechanics govern; redesign does not contradict).**

## P-14: BOARD/VG MODES must express inseparability — co-movement cannot be omitted in any play mode

**Violation test:** Does any play mode allow thread operations without three-dimensional co-movement?

**Assessment: PASS.**

- **TTRPG:** Version C fires on every operation (temporal auto-effect, epistemic auto-effect, actual d6). No exemptions.
- **Board game:** Co-Movement Card drawn on every Thread order (Weave, Mend, Investigate, Harvest). Deck expanded to 18 cards including Mending-specific effects. All orders draw cards at Accounting regardless of degree.
- **Hybrid:** Personal Phase uses Version C. Strategic Phase uses Co-Movement Cards. Both fire. "Both count toward seasonal changes."

Mending (new operation) has its own co-movement profile in TTRPG (§9.9/P-19) and draws Co-Movement Cards in board game. No mode exempts Mending from co-movement.

The board game degree table (P-23) includes Co-Movement Card draws on Partial results for Weave orders. Even partial results trigger co-movement.

FR Lock chronic drift is registered on territory cards in board game and hybrid, ensuring that Lock consequences propagate across all modes.

✓ **No violation.**

# PART FIFTEEN: THREAD OPERATION CATALOG

Thread operations are generated from first principles using the Three-Step procedure (§5.1–5.3). This catalog provides representative examples across all operation types, scales, and game contexts. Use these to calibrate Ob expectations and illustrate what each operation type can achieve; **do not treat them as an exhaustive list.**

Practitioners describe intent; the GM sets Ob from Diagnosis, scale, and actualization level. Any effect consistent with the operation's philosophical direction (§5.0) is legitimate. These entries are worked examples, not a fixed spell list.

**Terminology:** Coherence (Coh) = practitioner degradation track (10→0). RS = Rendering Stability (world track, 100→0). Co-movement fires on every operation per P-01.

**TS minimums by scale (authoritative, per §5.1):**

| Scale | Min TS | Base Ob (Weaving) | Base Ob (FR) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Object | 30 | 1 | 4 |
| Personal | 30 | 2 | 5 |
| Relational | 50 | 3 | 6 |
| Territorial | 50 | 4 | 7 |
| Structural | 70 | 5 | 8+ |

**TS Mastery Bonus:** For every full 20 TS above the operation's minimum TS requirement, +1 to the operation's primary numerical effect (Health restored, damage bonus, Cohesion modifier, duration step, strain reduced, etc.). Cap: +3. At TS 100: cap removed, +2 additional (total +5 at Object/Personal scale, +4 at Relational, +3 at Structural). This represents deeper configurational engagement — the practitioner's sensitivity permits qualitatively superior interaction with the threads, not merely higher success probability.

| Practitioner TS | vs Min TS 30 | vs Min TS 50 | vs Min TS 70 |
|---|---|---|---|
| 30 | +0 | — | — |
| 50 | +1 | +0 | — |
| 70 | +2 | +1 | +0 |
| 90 | +3 (cap) | +2 | +1 |
| 100 | +5 (cap removed, +2) | +4 | +3 |

*Example: W-01 (Wound Closure, min TS 30). At TS 30: restore up to 3 Health. At TS 50: 4. At TS 70: 5. At TS 90: 6. At TS 100: 8. A Resonant practitioner closing a wound is operating on a configuration almost entirely above the waterline — the intelligible-to-unintelligible ratio is so favorable that their mastery translates directly into effect magnitude.*

The pool already scales via TPS (more dice = higher Overwhelming chance). The Mastery Bonus scales the effect floor — what a given degree of success actually delivers. Both axes reward TS growth independently.

# 15.11 CANON COMPLIANCE NOTES

All operations in this catalog satisfy:

- **P-01 (Inseparability):** Every operation produces co-movement (temporal + epistemic + actual auto-effects). No exceptions.
- **P-02 (Ein Sof = fullness):** Gaps are excess, not void. Monstrous entities are rendering failures, not evil.
- **P-04 (Monstrosity ≠ moral):** No operation assigns moral valence to targets.
- **P-07 (Calamity = rendered-side):** All consequences are substrate strain from practitioner action, not ground responsiveness.
- **P-08 (Epistemological barrier):** No operation grants Thread-level knowledge to non-sensitives through study alone.
- **P-09 (Memory pulling = messy):** Memory displacement produces orphaned configurations, detectable by Diagnosis.
- **P-11 (Co-movement universal):** Zero-consequence operations are impossible under Version C.
- **P-14 (All modes express inseparability):** Board game orders produce Co-Movement Cards; TTRPG produces Version C auto-effects; hybrid produces both.

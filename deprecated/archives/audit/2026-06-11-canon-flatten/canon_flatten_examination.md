## В§0.5 Adversarial Reconciliation Layer (v2)

### Extended method
v1 produced the register from a single read pass. v2 attacks it. The protocol, applied to every P1:
1. **Restate the claim** exactly as committed вҖ” including its remediation, since a wrong remediation is the real cost.
2. **Construct the designer's strongest defense** вҖ” the most credible reading under which the flagged thing is *intended*, *already handled*, or *not actually a contradiction*. (This is where merged-but-distinct quantities and deliberate effects get rescued.)
3. **Test against raw repository bytes** вҖ” re-fetch and slice the actual file text with no regex transform of content, so the evidence is the file, not my paraphrase or my earlier grep. Embed the literal quote.
4. **Check ratification, not just presence** вҖ” a "strike" is only real if it is in a ratification store (`patch_register_active.yaml` for PP-NNN, `editorial_ledger.jsonl` for ED-NNN) or asserted by a *live* canonical doc. 5. **Issue a verdict:** SURVIVES В· STRENGTHENED В· REVERSED В· WITHDRAWN(false), with the corrected remediation.
A finding that fails any step is corrected in place and labelled вҖ” never silently dropped (`honest_findings`). A *candidate* finding that fails before it is logged is recorded as rejected (so the absence is auditable), not omitted.

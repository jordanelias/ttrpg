- C-4: The Church's position is historically grounded, morally intuitive, and sincerely held. The Church is not a conspiracy. It is a finite institution drawing a reasonable but wrong conclusion from real evidence.

## Confrontation with Excess Being (Phenomenology)

- P-1: There is NO FAILURE in the person confronting excess being. The rendering's finitude is constitutive, not a defect. A cup is not failing when you pour the ocean into it.
- P-2: The experience is the discovery that you are a process — that the rendering IS a rendering, that reality-as-experienced is constituted, not given
- P-3: The emotional register is ontological — the felt sense of being finite in the presence of the infinite — neither negative nor positive but prior to both
- P-4: What remains after confrontation is emotional residue (primitive-system trace) and fragmentary perceptions, not because of damage but because the rendering's constitutive scope was exceeded and storable experience was never fully constituted

## The Leap Mechanism

- L-1: The Leap suspends layer 2 (self-rendering). Exposed threads form involuntary knots along operational channels (the Leap is directional, not random)
- L-2: What propagates back through knots depends on the OPERATION TYPE:
  - Restorative (Mending, healing): aligned with substrate's natural tendency → feedback is stabilizing → NO inherent Coherence risk. This is why Wardens can work for decades.
  - Manipulative (reconfiguring to non-natural patterns): working against substrate tendency → substrate resistance propagates back → Coherence risk proportional to scale and deviation
  - Destructive (tearing, severing): extreme → catastrophic Coherence risk. This is what the Einhir mass working became when it failed.
- L-3: The Einhir Catastrophe was caused by a MANIPULATIVE/FOUNDATIONAL operation that failed and inverted — not by Mending. The Church's condemnation conflates all threadwork because it can't perceive the distinction between restoration and manipulation.
- L-4: Leap-formed knots are permanent because they form at substrate depth the self-rendering cannot access. They are bidirectional — practitioner feels changes in what they're knotted to, and practitioner's state affects what they're knotted to.
- L-5: TS development through confrontation is SAFE (growth, not pathology). The Leap is where Coherence risk lives. These are categorically different. The Church conflates them.

## Warden Reality / MS Dynamics

- W-1: Wardens' Coherence erodes from ENVIRONMENTAL EXPOSURE (Gaps, incursions, oversaturated Southernmost environment), NOT from Mending operations
- W-2: Mending is restorative — aligned with substrate tendency — and does not inherently cost Coherence
- W-3: Reality heals itself. The substrate's tendency toward coherence is constitutive and sourced from infinite positive being (Ein Sof). Even the Calamity wound would close eventually — on a timescale of millennia, not centuries.
- W-4: The Wardens ACCELERATE natural healing. They provide structural support at the wound margin where natural healing is slowest, enabling recovery at human-relevant timescales instead of geological ones.
- W-5: MS trajectory is a saw-tooth: the Wardens push MS up; colonial disruption, revolts, wars push it down; the substrate's natural tendency provides a floor under rendered-world disruption (surface damage heals relatively quickly). The Wardens have achieved MS 72 DESPITE revolts, 177 years of colonial oppression, and a secession war.
- W-6: MS 72 is a high-water mark. The powderkeg setting is about to test it.
- W-7: Only direct substrate operations (threadwork through the Leap — specifically manipulative/destructive operations) can cause rapid, large MS drops. Rendered-world events (war, political collapse) cause small drops that the substrate's natural tendency recovers.

## MS and Calamity Radiation

- R-1: MS bands (Stable 100-80, Strained 79-60, Fragile 59-40, Fractured 39-20, Critical 19-1, Rupture 0) map to radiation effects at each node distance from T15
- R-2: At game start MS 72 (Strained): only T15 and distance-1 territories (T6, T13) experience effects. Rest of peninsula is Normal.
- R-3: If MS drops below 60 (Fragile): distance-2 territories (T5, T12) start experiencing anomalies
- R-4: If MS drops below 40 (Fractured): effects reach distance-3 (Crown heartland, T1, T14)
- R-5: The MS trajectory traced against radiation tells the peninsula's entire history — which territories experienced what effects during which era, shaping cultural attitudes, institutional character, and the caste system's experiential basis
- R-6: Secession Wars and Calamity radiation have INVERTED geographic profiles — war damage concentrated in the north (T3, T14, T1, T2, T10), Calamity damage concentrated in the south (T15, T6, T13). Peninsula damaged from both ends.
- R-7: Himmelenger (T9) emerged from the Secession Wars with thread-lattice largely intact due to negotiated neutrality — this gives the Church a material stability advantage that it attributes to Solmund's grace. The evidence is real. The explanation is wrong.

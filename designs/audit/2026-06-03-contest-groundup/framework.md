# Social Contest — Ground-Up Framework (from the rhetoric corpus)

Designed fresh from the historical corpus as the top-down source. No v30 reference: no Memory/Projection, no "genre" axis, no Evidence-clock, no inherited formulas or trackers. The corpus validates *structure*, never numbers; the metaphysics/world/characters are untouched; the proposed modes are Jordan-vetoable.

## A. The primitives the contest needs (what recurs across every tradition)

1. **Terrain diagnosis (stasis).** Before arguing you locate what the dispute turns on, because arguments fit to one kind of question are useless on another. Four grounds: fact ("did it happen"), definition ("what is it" — the reframe), quality ("was it justified"), jurisdiction ("should this be heard here, now" — the venue-challenge). The defence runs as a strongest-tenable-rung fallback ladder: deny the act → contest its label → justify it → challenge the venue.

2. **Three modes of proof (the per-move axis).** Ethos (the speaker's projected standing), pathos (the audience's state), logos (the argument itself). This is the tactical choice each exchange, not a flavor layer.

3. **Standing as a spendable resource.** Character/standing is a resource you build, spend, and can have stripped — cross-traditionally the most robust single primitive. Form itself is rank-indexed: who you are to whom constrains what you may say and how.

4. **The judge is the target, not the opponent.** You move the adjudicator; the opponent is only the obstacle. The adjudicator's nature conditions what lands.

5. **Self-gating (which tactics are licit).** What you may legitimately do is bounded by your standing relative to the opponent *and* the assembly's composition — a triage (opponent superior/equal/inferior × audience learned/ignorant × friendly/hostile) that decides whether honest argument is owed or harder devices are licensed. Reaching for an illicit device is itself a way to lose.

6. **Thesis vs counter-thesis exchange.** The structured back-and-forth (claim against counter-claim) is the unit of play.

7. **Defeat-conditions (the loss catalogue).** An enumerable set of named faults — self-contradiction, evasion, non-responsiveness, falling silent when pressed, resorting to a barred device — any of which can *end* the contest against the side that incurs it. Adjudicated against a checklist, deterministically; not a knife-edge roll. This is a resolution path distinct from "accumulate to a threshold."

8. **A merits accumulation where no fault ends it.** Where neither side clinches by a defeat-condition, the merits build over the exchanges toward a decision — the contest as a deepening accumulation, not a single swing.

9. **A staged turn shape.** Invention → arrangement → delivery: a move is composed and committed in stages, which is the natural shape of a turn-structured resolution.

## B. The modes of discourse (distinct contests — they do not share a win condition)

The single most important inheritance: persuasion is not one activity but several, separated by *who judges and what they decide*. Each is its own game.

- **Deliberative (assembly / council).** A body decides *future action*; the question is the advantageous and the right. A framing authority poses the question; speaking order is standing-indexed; the body decides by a count. Win = the body resolves for your motion.

- **Forensic (trial / tribunal).** A judge weighs a *past act*; the question is just/unjust. Runs on the stasis defence ladder plus a graded weight-of-proof structure (charge → issue joined → proof accrues → verdict). Win = the verdict.

- **Ceremonial (praise / blame).** An audience as spectator in the present; nothing is *at issue* — the opposite of a contested case. It builds or spends standing rather than resolving a dispute. Win = a standing shift / acclamation.

- **Dyadic counsel (private audience).** You read and steer *one* powerful listener; success is invisible (the course seems self-chosen by the listener). Scan the listener's power and disposition, then open or close, in a register of his desire or his fear, matched to his character. This is a genuinely different art from public contest and likely a distinct sub-system, not the same engine with the audience turned down.

- **Royal appeal (petition).** Asymmetric, *up* a power gradient. Two instruments, not one: the **supplique** (subject → sovereign, seeking grace; rank-indexed form, deference register, an intercessor's standing modifies success) and the **remontrance** (an institution → Crown, contesting a measure before it takes effect — a legitimated check with its own escalation ladder). Win = grace granted, or the measure reconsidered.

- **Negotiation (treaty / alliance).** A bounded, reputation-laden exchange between parties: typed envoys with typed latitude, escalating instruments (conciliation → concession → sowing division → force), resolved within a small set of standing policy stances. Topologically distinct from debate. Win = an agreement inside the overlap of interests.

"Trial" is the forensic mode; "parliament" the deliberative; each needs its own framing authority, decision rule, and clock — they are not skins on one contest.

## C. How these resolve (consistent with the resolver the diagnostic settled)

Each mode decomposes into a deterministic frame around a small stochastic core. The institution is the deterministic wrapper — who frames the question, who may speak in what order, the merits clock, the defeat-condition checklist, the standing ledger — and the stochastic surface is only the reception of a given move before a given audience. Two resolution paths sit inside that frame: a **deterministic clinch** when a side incurs a named defeat-condition, and a **merits accumulation** otherwise, fed by the bounded reception of each exchange. This is the composite the resolution diagnostic concluded was appropriate, and the corpus reaches the same shape independently.

## What stays out of scope here
Numbers (lever magnitudes, resource sizes, thresholds) are sim-validation questions, not historical ones — the corpus grounds the structure only. The modes beyond the core debate (royal appeal, negotiation, the dyadic/public split as separate sub-systems) are proposals for Jordan's call, not decisions.

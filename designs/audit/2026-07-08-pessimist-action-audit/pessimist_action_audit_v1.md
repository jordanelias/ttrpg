The lone condemned finding (*Concentration* as a dominant-strategy risk) was **overturned to KEEP** by the critic: its win-rate deficit is the named counter working as intended, not a flaw.
- **SC — near-whole.** 14/17 KEEP. The real bite is **KU-1**: Recall (+5D) + Prep (+3D) + uncapped Corroborate (+1D) sum to +9D on a 12–18D pool with no combined ceiling stated anywhere (REFINE, independently corroborated by the 2026-07-04 refutation's L-A). *Appraise* REFINEs (narrowed to venue-fixed proceedings). - **FA — layering is not duplication.** The three "unreconciled vocabularies" resolve cleanly: da.* is the substrate's normal Key-routing layer (REFINE to a crosswalk table, not a duplicate menu); the constructive levers, treaty family, and March/Conquest all KEEP. The **punitive Parliamentary cluster (5 rows) DISTILLs to one parameterized action** — its own "Combined Embargo+Blockade" is the design's textual proof of compositionality. *Thread Operation*-as-FA-action was **downgraded PRUNE→REFINE**: a legitimate FA/TW interface point, single-source the mechanics to TW.
- **SE — the only hard deletions live here.** *Trade* PRUNE (a bare income roll with zero settlement/faction/NPC effect, unlike every sibling; corroborated by module_contracts' own settlement_economy retirement flag) and *Grant/Revoke Subnational* PRUNE (lane-homing: it is a Provincial-Authority Domain Action, belongs in FA). The *Sponsor* MERGE resolves a genuine dominant-strategy bug — §9's no-downside flat version strictly dominates the Debt-bearing redesign version.

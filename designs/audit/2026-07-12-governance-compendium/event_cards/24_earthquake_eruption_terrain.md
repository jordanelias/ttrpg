# 2.4 Earthquake, Eruption, Terrain-Altering Events (Geographical/Climatic)

*Key: `catastrophic-terrain-events-mechanical-distillation`. The through-line: **a Crisis card is a retroactive audit of a Fortify/Develop method skipped seasons earlier** (Rebuild-to-Code, Housing Code, Water Infrastructure, Relocate) — and the *disaster becomes the political lever*, not just the loss.*

### HEV-QUAKE-01 — 1755 Lisbon Earthquake/Tsunami/Fire (Lisbon, 1755) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1755 Lisbon Earthquake/Tsunami/Fire — *§1.3/§1.6/§2.1–2.3.*
- **Trigger:** `Π≥8 AND Weight≥high AND no Rebuild-to-Code tag AND a feast/high-attendance crowd multiplier active`
- **Response branches:** Comply (triage+price controls: Treasury−−, Order+1, Precedent); Bargain (let Church/nobility fund: their Standing+1); Defy (Prosperity−−, `Abandoned`, Π pegged)
- **Follow-on (FO):** Comply seeds a multi-season Develop reconstruction (durable Fortify tag) + an Intrigue if Church Standing was curtailed to pay for it.
- **Introduces (Action):** Rebuild-to-Code (new Fortify method lowering future Earthquake/Fire trigger weight+severity; pre-emptive or as Comply's follow-on).
- **Loop:** No Rebuild-to-Code = higher draw-weight/severity; when the Crisis fires, Comply's political cover lets the governor curtail a patron's Standing (Precedent) to fund the rebuild, raising that faction's Grudge and seeding Intrigue — the disaster is the lever.

### HEV-QUAKE-02 — 1783–84 Laki Eruption / Móðuharðindin (Iceland, 1783–84) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1783–84 Laki Eruption / Móðuharðindin — *§1.3a Compact ED-SE-0019, §1.4, §1.6.*
- **Trigger:** `Π≥7 AND active fixed-extraction Compact (closed-monopoly/Encabezamiento lock) AND Prosperity falling 2+ seasons`
- **Response branches:** Comply (ease terms + relief grain, Compact loosened, Π−2); Bargain (terms unchanged, drifts `Neglected`); Defy (permanent Prosperity-floor drop, Grudge(Crown))
- **Follow-on (FO):** Defy seeds a "population petitions to relocate/emigrate" Ambition that can stall indefinitely if never funded (the stalled Jutland-resettlement proposal).
- **Introduces (Action):** Renegotiate Compact (Treat/Levy: emergency-break an active extraction Compact mid-term, at a Precedent other settlements can cite).
- **Loop:** A fixed-rate Compact prevents Prosperity from absorbing a shock, so any live Compact raises famine-Crisis weight+duration; Comply forces the holder to loosen terms (Π down now) but sets a Precedent other Compact-bound settlements invoke via Petition later.

### HEV-QUAKE-03 — 1556 Jiajing (Shaanxi) Earthquake (Shaanxi, 1556) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1556 Jiajing (Shaanxi) Earthquake — *§1.3 Fortify, §1.4, Part 7.*
- **Trigger:** `Π≥8 AND no Fortify housing method AND Weight≥high`
- **Response branches:** Comply (edict, officials, food aid, tax-debt cancellation — Debt tags cleared); Bargain (thin aid Precedent); Defy (PS−−−, `Forsaken`)
- **Follow-on (FO):** If the ruler's Court Attendance is low during resolution, seeds an Intrigue where a Ministry/Consulta figure's Standing rises unchecked.
- **Introduces (Action):** Housing Code (Fortify method regulating high-density dwelling, lowering casualty multiplier — the pre-emptive mirror of Rebuild-to-Code).
- **Loop:** Skipping it leaves the structural floor low (higher weight + casualties); because Comply needs Treasury a distracted low-Court-Attendance ruler is slow to authorize, the crisis becomes the occasion for a delegated Ministry actor's Standing to climb.

### HEV-QUAKE-04 — 1923 Great Kanto Earthquake (Kanto, Japan, 1923) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1923 Great Kanto Earthquake — *§1.3 Develop fork/Force, §1.6 Grudge/Outlawed, Rival Cohort dimension.*
- **Trigger:** `Π≥9 AND Weight≥high AND a resident Rival Cohort/outsider dimension present`
- **Response branches:** Comply (full reconstruction: Guild Influence claimant seeded); Bargain (budget slashed ~⅔, `underbuilt capital`); Defy (Prosperity collapsed)
- **Follow-on (FO):** If Keep Order was resolved via Force, seeds rumor-driven vigilante violence against the Rival Cohort (Grudge + Outlawed tag).
- **Introduces (Action):** Force (Keep Order) Crisis-tier violence branch (explicit high-Π downside: mob violence against the cohort, beyond the normal PS/Disposition cost).
- **Loop:** High-severity Crisis + unaddressed Rival Cohort makes Force riskier (a chance to spawn a pogrom Intrigue); the Develop funding fork chosen afterward (full vs Corvée-cut) sets the Prosperity/Defense floor for the campaign — a one-time Crisis into a durable trajectory.

### HEV-QUAKE-05 — 1669 Etna Eruption / Catania Lava Diversion (Catania, Sicily, 1669) · Friction
- **Historical grounding:** The 1669 Etna Eruption / Catania Lava Diversion — *§1.3 Treat, §1.3a Compact.*
- **Trigger:** `a Crisis:Eruption affects Settlement A AND adjacent B has no standing Treat/Compact covering emergency intervention`
- **Response branches:** Comply (endorse B's unilateral diversion: A spared, B's Disposition collapses, Grudge(A↔B)); Bargain (broker compensation → bounded Compact, both Dispositions near-neutral); Defy (A takes full damage)
- **Follow-on (FO):** Comply-without-Bargain seeds a Friction/Intrigue between the two settlements' factions (Paterno's ~500 inhabitants halting the diverted flow).
- **Introduces (Action):** Broker Diversion (Treat: mediate between two settlements over a live Crisis, converting a unilateral act into a negotiated Compact).
- **Loop:** Without a pre-existing Treat/Compact link a live eruption resolves unilaterally, writing an inter-settlement Grudge raising future Friction weight; spending AP on Treat converts the shock into a bounded Compact — AP now for lower Π in both later.

### HEV-QUAKE-06 — 1815 Tambora / 1816 Year Without a Summer (global, 1815–16) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1815 Tambora / 1816 Year Without a Summer — *§1.4, §1.3 Sponsor, settlement Weight collapse.*
- **Trigger:** `a province-wide Π shock modifier active (multiple settlements simultaneously) AND at-risk settlements cross-referenced by low banked Disposition/Treasury`
- **Response branches:** Comply (province-wide famine-relief Directive: Treasury−−− province-wide, Debt forgiven); Bargain (ad hoc local relief, uneven); Defy (Π max, permanent Prosperity drop + an illicit-economy tag echoing opium-cultivation displacement)
- **Follow-on (FO):** A zero-investment settlement's Weight collapses toward zero (exits play); Defy elsewhere seeds a Crisis→Crisis into open revolt.
- **Introduces (Action):** Famine Relief Directive (province-wide variant) / Open Emergency Granary (Sponsor method pre-funding a Prosperity floor).
- **Loop:** Banked Disposition/Treasury from prior Sponsor/Develop decides whether a province-wide shock is absorbed via cheap Bargain or forces costly Comply; a zero-investment settlement is removed from play, and Defy anywhere permanently reshapes that economy (illicit-crop tag) lowering future Order and raising Crisis/Intrigue weight.

### HEV-QUAKE-07 — 1906 San Francisco Earthquake & Fire (San Francisco, 1906) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1906 San Francisco Earthquake & Fire — *§1.3 Fortify Walls/Force, faction Standing/Reputation.*
- **Trigger:** `Π≥8 AND no water-infrastructure Fortify tag AND a Force-heavy Keep Order history flag (corrupt-governance marker)`
- **Response branches:** Comply (martial law/shoot-on-sight: Order++ fast, Grudge); Bargain (elite reconstruction committee via a Consulta body, governor control loosens); Defy (fire consumes the settlement)
- **Follow-on (FO):** A resident Guild present at relief distribution can pay "in full" (Reputation `Honored their word`) or invoke technicalities (Grudge + `Weaseled`) — either chaining into that faction's Standing trajectory.
- **Introduces (Action):** Water Infrastructure (Fortify Walls extension covering water-mains) / Guild Relief (faction-side Sponsor act banking Reputation on the faction's own ladder).
- **Loop:** Skipping infrastructure raises fire-following-earthquake severity; how the Crisis resolves differentially shifts a resident Guild's Standing (full-relief vs exclusions), changing its future willingness to fund the governor's Develop/Fortify requests.

### HEV-QUAKE-08 — 1693 Val di Noto Earthquake (Val di Noto, Sicily, 1693) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 1693 Val di Noto Earthquake — *settlement_layer Charter/Quo Warranto, §1.3 Develop.*
- **Trigger:** `Π≥8 AND Weight below a survivability floor AND Charter held by a feudal/ecclesiastical actor`
- **Response branches:** Comply (full relocation "New [Settlement]", gated by Charter-holder consent via Quo Warranto: multi-season sink, Prosperity ceiling raised, `planned capital` Precedent); Bargain (partial rebuild in place, lower ceiling, faster); Defy (Weight stays collapsed)
- **Follow-on (FO):** A successful relocation seeds a multi-season Develop build chain + a `Heritage Town` Reputation raising future Sponsor yield.
- **Introduces (Action):** Relocate Settlement (heavy multi-season Develop method gated by Charter control via Quo Warranto).
- **Loop:** Whether relocation is even available is gated by Charter control, so the settlement_layer's ownership machinery directly determines the Crisis's options; relocating trades heavy up-front AP/Treasury for a durable Prosperity-ceiling increase + a Reputation tag raising every future Sponsor's return.

### HEV-QUAKE-09 — 79 AD Vesuvius (Pompeii/Herculaneum, 79 AD) · Crisis
- **Historical grounding:** The 79 AD Vesuvius eruption — *§2.1–2.3 escalate, Part 7 commissioners-by-lot.*
- **Trigger:** `a prior Friction "recurring tremors" card was ignored 2+ seasons AND Π at the escalate ceiling`
- **Response branches:** Comply (emergency Directive, Consulta commissioners by lot, relief partly funded via escheat of the heirless dead); Bargain (slower partial relief); Defy (Crown Reputation collapse)
- **Follow-on (FO):** Maximum severity can zero the Weight permanently ("erasure") + a cautionary Precedent other settlements cite when a similar tremor card is drawn ("we warned you").
- **Introduces (Action):** Evacuate (new emergency sub-verb: spend AP ahead of a telegraphed Crisis to relocate population, converting erasure to survivable-with-losses).
- **Loop:** Ignoring a tremor warning raises its escalate-weight toward a Weight-zeroing Crisis; early Evacuate converts that to a survivable branch, at the recurring cost of committing scarce AP against a threat that most seasons doesn't land — warning-fatigue made mechanical.

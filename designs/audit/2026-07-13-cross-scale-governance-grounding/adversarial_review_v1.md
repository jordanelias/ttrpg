  rejects EU4 disasters as single-funnel accumulators, and Court-and-Country *is* one (a monthly-ticking
  progress bar firing at 100). Its name ("Court"=top, "Country"=bottom) reads like a two-signal collision;
  the mechanism isn't. It is **not in the research files** — the catalog introduced it. **Replace with
  Imperator Tyranny × Aggressive-Expansion** (a top-down and a bottom-up stress drawing the *same* fragility
  gauge simultaneously — the real two-signal game example, already in `game_precedents G7`). **E1's primitive
  survives** on Mandate-resonance + DF mandate-clock + Imperator Tyranny×AE.
- **§3.3 · Roman auctoritas/potestas → L-vs-Mandate — DOWNGRADED to confirmatory-vocabulary.** The
  transferable mechanism (borrow legitimacy once at the apex, run an independent power-chain beneath) is
  carried by **Tokugawa** (the dated, co-signed 1615 Kinchū narabi), **not Rome**. The Roman citation
  mis-equates *auctoritas* (elite/personal prestige, top-down) with L (bottom-up popular consent); *potestas*
  →Mandate is the closer fit. `legitimacy_precedents §6`'s own caveat ("a *distinction*, not a rigid working
  vocabulary — retrospective systematization") was dropped and should be restored. **Fix stands on Tokugawa.**
- **§3.4 · anacyclosis → decay — DOWNGRADED to thematic.** Anacyclosis (Polybius) is *qualitative regime-form
  succession*, not scalar mean-reversion. B4's `−λ_decay·(L−baseline)` term is a real scalar-decay fix, but
  its load-bearing precedents are **Old World cognomen-decay** (50%→…→10%) and **EU4 Horde Unity** (constant
  unconditional decay); anacyclosis is a shared-*theme*-of-decline analogy, not a mechanism transfer. B4
  (a chain-completion on OF-3 `decay()`) is unaffected.
- **P-01 / P-12 / P-14 citations (B2, E1, E2, G2) — REMOVE.** Per their Violation-Test language in
  `canon/02_canon_constraints.md`, these constraints are textually scoped to **Thread-operation / Coherence
  resolution**; none of these governance fixes are thread-ops, so citing them as compliance cover is an
  unearned category error. **The applicable and correctly-applied framework (GD-1/2/3, holonic top-imposed/
  bottom-up-emergent, no-GM) stands** — the compliance verdicts do not change, only the decorative citations.
- **Minor loose-but-low-stakes** (confirmatory-only, attached to chain-completions; note, don't lean):

governance is unbuilt wiring on sound logic (~21 chain-completions); the genuinely-new mechanism surface is
~6–8 items, and it is worth building precisely because it is small and load-bearing.**

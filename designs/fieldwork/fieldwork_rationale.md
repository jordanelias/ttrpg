## §13 DESIGN RATIONALE INDEX

### §13.1 Core Naming: Intelligibility Gradient (not "Rendering Gradient")

The gradient describes variation in **substrate accessibility** — how much of the Thread substrate is intelligible to a given consciousness — not variation in the rendering operation itself. Rendering is the operation; intelligibility is the property of the substrate relative to the observer. "Rendering Gradient" would imply the gradient is in the rendering process, which contradicts the Foundations: rendering is a constant (P-01 — it cannot be switched off), while what the rendering can process varies. "Intelligibility Gradient" names what actually varies: how much of the substrate exceeds the rendering frame at a given depth.

### §13.2 Rendering Strain vs Co-Movement

**Why rendering strain fires on passive Depth 3+ encounter, but co-movement is reserved for Thread-Read:**

P-01 (Inseparability) specifies co-movement as the consequence of Thread *manipulation* — "manipulations of threads" cause substrate co-movement. Passive perception, even of Depth 3+ content, is not a Thread manipulation. A character observing an Einhir Remnant is not performing an operation on the Thread substrate; they are perceiving something the substrate presents to them. Rendering strain (Coherence pressure, Certainty shift) is appropriate for the perceptual encounter; co-movement would overextend P-01 into perceptual territory it was not designed to govern.

Thread-Read is the exception precisely because it is a perceptive Leap — an active Thread operation packaged as investigation. The Leap is a Thread manipulation (extending Thread contact into the investigative domain). P-01 fires on Thread-Read because Thread-Read IS a Thread operation.

### §13.3 Cover as Derived Value (Cognition + History)

Cover parallels the Health and Composure structure: both are derived values that set thresholds before a tracked resource (Exposure) produces consequences. The Cognition + History formula was selected because:

1. **Cognition** governs analytical capacity — knowing how to maintain a cover identity, recognise exposure risks, adapt behaviour under observation.
2. **History** points reflect learned tradecraft — a character with a "Traveller" or "Merchant" History has a deeper, more convincing cover persona than one without.
3. The formula parallels Stamina (Endurance + 1) and Composure (Charisma + 6): derived from the attribute most relevant to the capacity, modified by a constant that sets the meaningful range.

Alternative considered: Attunement-based Cover (reading the social environment to avoid detection). Rejected: Cover is about maintaining a stable false identity, not about reading others. Attunement governs the social *read*; Cognition governs the *construction* of the cover.

### §13.4 Sincerity Gate (Spirit, not Attunement)

The Sincerity Gate fires when a character attempts Connect or Converse for purely instrumental reasons — when they are not genuinely attempting to deepen the relationship. Spirit was chosen over Attunement because:

The gate is about the character's *own* sincerity — their willingness to make the relationship real — not their capacity to perceive the NPC's state. Attunement governs the *read*; Spirit governs resolve, commitment, and the willingness to be genuine. Spirit (= Resolve = the attribute of conviction) is the correct mechanical expression of "are you actually trying to connect, or are you running a social manipulation?"

### §13.5 Desperate Trail: 3 Consecutive Failures

Three consecutive failures before escalation was calibrated to:
- Be rare enough that it doesn't trigger constantly (3D investigation pool at TN 7, Ob 2: P(failure) ≈ 25% → P(three consecutive) ≈ 1.6%)
- Be frequent enough to matter (high-stakes investigators at unusual Obs face it more often)
- Match the narrative sense of a stalled investigation reaching crisis point

The Desperate Trail response (TN 8 + doubled Exposure + GM complication + Partial improves to +2) follows the Fail Forward principle: the situation *escalates* rather than stalls. Progress remains possible — a 4D pool at TN 8 has P(≥1 success) ≈ 60% — but the cost of failure is higher and the environment becomes more hostile. The +2 Partial improvement compensates for the increased difficulty at the margin.

### §13.6 Survey BG Action: Ob = (5 − Proximity Rating) + 1

The inverse-Proximity formula was chosen because:
- **Design intent:** Northern territories (far from Calamity, high Proximity Rating) are safer and more legible. A Survey in Lowenskyst (Proximity 5, far north) is Ob 1 — straightforward reconnaissance of settled, stable land.
- **Mechanical fit:** Askeheim (T15, Proximity 0) is Ob 6 — the Calamity epicentre is existentially dangerous to survey. The formula produces the correct intuition: danger scales with proximity to Calamity.
- **Balance check (SIM-DEBT-FW-05, RESOLVED PP-583):** Survey and Govern occupy different niches. Govern dominates mid-proximity territories (reliable Prosperity development). Survey dominates high-proximity safe northern territories (discovery without risk). Neither dominates the other.

### §13.7 Exposure → AP Cap (+1/character/season, +2/territory/season)

The cap prevents fieldwork from becoming a primary driver of TC (Church Attention) escalation. Calibration (SIM-DEBT-FW-04, RESOLVED PP-581): fieldwork contributes approximately 11% of maximum TC acceleration over 4 seasons under the cap. This is bounded to insignificance relative to primary TC drivers (Thread operations, Church territorial conflict, Excommunication chains). The cap ensures fieldwork feels consequential at the personal scale while remaining strategically minor at the faction scale.

### §13.8 Thread-Read as Perceptive Leap

Thread-Read was defined as the only fieldwork action that triggers co-movement because it is the only fieldwork action that constitutes a Thread operation. The design decision to route Thread-based investigation through the Leap mechanism (rather than a separate Thread investigation operation) was made to:

1. **Preserve the Thread operation taxonomy.** Creating a "Thread Investigation" operation would require a new category in the threadwork system. Thread-Read *is* a Thread contact operation that happens to serve investigative purposes — it fits cleanly into the existing Leap → Contact → Operation sequence.
2. **Enforce the epistemological barrier (P-08).** Thread-Read produces Inert Knowledge for non-sensitive receivers. This is only coherent if Thread-Read is a Thread operation with Thread-operation consequences. A non-operation "Thread sense" would bypass P-08.
3. **Keep the perceptual economy consistent.** TS 30+ is required for Thread-Read because Thread contact requires TS 30+ for Leap. The investigation capacity flows from the same threshold as the operational capacity.

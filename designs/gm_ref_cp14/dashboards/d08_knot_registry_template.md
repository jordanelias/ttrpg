## ANCHORING SCENE PROCEDURE
*Use when recovering Coherence through relational connection.*

1. Scene must involve genuine engagement with this Knot's connected entity
2. Roll: Bonds, TN 7, Ob 2
3. Success: +1 Coherence; this Knot +1 strain (the recovery costs the relationship)
4. Failure: no Coherence recovery; no strain increase
5. Maximum: one Anchoring Scene per Knot per season

---

## STRAIN CONSEQUENCE NOTES
- Strain 1–2: narrative unease from connected entity; no mechanical effect
- Strain 3–4: connected entity behaves with distance or distress toward practitioner
- Strain 5 (breaking): Knot severs — connected entity's threads no longer knotted to practitioner's; relational consequence is permanent unless deliberately re-woven (Weaving Relational, Ob 3)

---

## CO-MOVEMENT KNOT PROPAGATION
*When practitioner undergoes Thread operations, Knots transmit a fraction of co-movement effects to connected entities.*

- **Strong Knot:** connected entity experiences roughly 1/3 of co-movement magnitude
- **Moderate Knot:** connected entity experiences faint version (narrative only in most cases)
- **Wrapping:** connected entity experiences trace only; requires TS 30+ to perceive

*The GM applies this as narrative texture, not a separate mechanical roll, unless the operation was at Relational+ scale targeting the Knot directly.*

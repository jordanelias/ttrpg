# PART TWO: THREADWORK AT KEY DECISION POINTS

## 2.1 The Thread Sensitivity Map as Political Geography

The caste system is not just a social hierarchy. It is a perceptual hierarchy.

| Territory band | TS baseline (non-practitioner population) | Church penetration | Forgetting resistance | Political status |
|---------------|------------------------------------------|-------------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| Northern (Valorsmark, T1, T8-T9) | 0-3 | Maximum | Minimal (Ob 4 Forgetting = fail) | Establishment. Crown/Hafenmark heartland. |
| Central (Hafenmark coastal, T5-T7) | 3-8 | High | Low | Parliamentary establishment. Baralta's base. |
| Southern Crown (T12-T14) | 8-15 | Medium | Moderate | Instrumental territories. Administered, not represented. |
| Varfell (T1-T4) | 12-25 | Low | Significant | Stigmatised duchy. Southern Einhir population. Vaynard's base. |
| Southernmost approach (T13, T15) | 20-40 | Minimal | High | Warden territory. Thread-active. Forgetting boundary. |

**This distribution means:**
- Thread operations are most likely in the south. Church Heresy Investigations disproportionately target the south. The enforcement pattern is ethnic.
- The Forgetting is weakest in the south. Southern populations retain more knowledge of the Southernmost crisis. Northern populations forget completely.
- Vaynard's political coalition problem is structural: the people who can perceive Thread reality (southerners with TS 12-25) are the people the settlement excludes from political participation (caste). The people who hold political power (northerners with TS 0-3) cannot perceive Thread reality.

**The Thread Sensitivity map IS the political map.** The settlement suppresses the populations that can perceive what the settlement is built to deny.

## 2.2 Almud's Discovery Event — Threadwork Analysis

**Current state:** Almud TS 0. Northern Einhir. Fully essentialist-formed. No Thread perception.

**Trigger:** PCs bring Thread reality to Valorsplatz (T1 — maximum Church penetration, TS baseline 0-3). This requires a Thread operation visible to TS 0 observers, which means: a Failure that produces a rendering anomaly (Shifting Object, Gap symptom, environmental distortion), or RS degradation sufficient that non-practitioners experience rendering failures (RS ≤ 39, Fractured band).

**TS growth check on trigger:**
- Pool: Spirit (unknown — needs stat block definition)
- TN: 7
- Ob: 2 (essentialist formation)
- At Spirit 3 (estimated for Almud): 3D vs Ob 2, TN 7. P(Success) ≈ 15%. P(Failure) ≈ 65%.

**Expected outcome: Failure.** Almud witnesses something he cannot process. Like Himlensendt at the originary Lock, the experience does not produce Thread Sensitivity. It produces an unfileable memory — something happened, he knows it was significant, he cannot say what it was. The Forgetting does not operate here (he is in Valorsplatz, not the Southernmost), but the essentialist formation functions as a perceptual barrier that produces the same effect: the event is real but his framework has no category for it.

**If Success (15%):** Almud gains minimal TS (+1 to +3). He is now TS 1-3 — still below all operational thresholds but no longer at zero. He can sense that something is present in Thread-significant environments. His ethical doubt about the caste becomes experiential: he is now what the caste stigmatises. The man who questioned whether the system was defensible is now on the wrong side of the system he questioned.

**Arc emergence from Discovery Event:**
- Failure path: Almud has an experience he cannot name. He becomes quieter on the Einhir question — not because his doubt resolved but because something happened that makes the question larger than his governance framework can hold. PCs who witnessed the same event know what happened. Almud does not. The information asymmetry between the PCs and the Crown is now experiential, not just political.
- Success path: Almud's governance calculus is permanently altered. He now has a personal stake in the Einhir question that his pragmatic framework did not anticipate. The ethical doubt becomes embodied. He cannot manage the caste as a line item because he is the line item.

**Interaction with Baralta's Crown claim:** If Almud's Discovery Event fires and he gains even minimal TS, the Church has theoretical grounds for Excommunication. A Thread-sensitive king on a throne consecrated by a Church that condemns Thread Sensitivity is a constitutional crisis. If Baralta is positioning for the Crown and Almud is exposed as Thread-sensitive, Baralta's case becomes overwhelming: the current monarch is theologically compromised, the deed-presumption is rebutted, and the deed-logic's strongest candidate (Baralta) should take over.

## 2.3 Baralta's Thread Foreclosure — Threadwork Analysis

**Baralta will never develop Thread Sensitivity.** This is not a design gap. It is the design.

Her sovereign divine right framework + categorical imperative + Henry VIII ecclesiology = no ontological space for Thread reality. Her essentialist formation is not just theological (Church-derived) but philosophical (categorical imperative rejects non-universalisable perception) and political (sovereign authority derives from Solmund's grace, not from pre-Solmund Thread traditions).

**Three barriers, not one.** Most characters have one barrier to TS development (essentialist formation, Ob +1). Baralta has three:
1. Essentialist formation (theological — same as all Church-culturally integrated characters)
2. Categorical imperative (philosophical — Thread perception is not universalisable, therefore not real in her framework)
3. Sovereign divine right (political — Thread reality delegitimises her authority claim)

**[FLAG: ED-417 — Baralta's triple-barrier to TS. Propose: Baralta's TS growth check (if it ever fires) is at Ob 4, not Ob 2. The additional barriers are not just flavour — they are mechanically real. At Ob 4, even Spirit 5 produces P(Success) ≈ 2%.]**

**Arc emergence from foreclosure:**
Baralta is the only ruler who will never perceive the Southernmost crisis. If she takes the Crown, the kingdom is governed by a person who is constitutionally incapable of understanding its existential threat. Her Crown would be the most competent governance the peninsula has ever seen — and it would be governing the wrong problem. She would subordinate the Church (good for TC), suppress the Restoration (bad for the caste), strengthen military defence (irrelevant to RS), and never once perceive that RS is degrading toward the Rupture.

**This is the tragedy of Baralta's candidacy.** The deed-logic says she should rule. Thread reality says she cannot see what ruling requires. The campaign's central irony: the most competent political actor on the peninsula is the least capable of addressing the peninsula's actual problem.

**Vaynard has environmental TS from southern Einhir baseline.** Estimated TS 15-25 (needs stat block — using TS 20 provisionally).

At TS 20, Vaynard:
- Can perceive Thread operations (detection threshold TS 10+)
- Can sense Thread-touched objects (threshold TS 20+, Dormant)
- Cannot Leap (requires TS 30+)
- Cannot perform operations
- Has +1D on Forgetting Checks (TS 20 ÷ 20 = 1 bonus die)

**The Forgetting as political prison (mechanical expression):**
Vaynard's testimony about the Southernmost in Appeals or Debates carries the Testimony Ob penalty: +1 Ob for Boundary exposure, +2 for Interior. Even with his TS 20 providing +1D on his own Forgetting Checks, when he tries to convince non-practitioners, THEIR Forgetting resistance determines whether his testimony registers.

A non-practitioner audience member (TS 0-5) hearing Vaynard's testimony about the Southernmost crisis:
- Forgetting Check: Spirit TN 7, Ob 3-4 (Deep Interior/Core exposure level if Vaynard is reporting from those zones)
- At Spirit 3, pool 3D: P(Success at Ob 3) ≈ 7%. P(Success at Ob 4) ≈ 1%.

**The audience literally cannot retain what Vaynard is telling them.** This is not a political failure. It is an epistemological one. The most important argument on the peninsula — "the world is dying and only Einhir knowledge can save it" — dissolves in the consciousness of anyone who hears it and lacks Thread Sensitivity.

**Arc emergence from the Forgetting prison:**
Vaynard's only path to political coalition is through practitioners. He needs people with TS 30+ who can testify without Forgetting degradation. This drives:
- His recruitment of Maret Uln (TS ~50)
- His interest in PC practitioners
- His intelligence operations in Thread-active zones (building a network of people who can perceive what he perceives)
- **His strategic interest in Lenneth's programme.** If Lenneth's Cultural Revival Track reaches 5+ (Thread work acknowledged), the institutional barrier to practitioner testimony drops. The Forgetting still operates, but the political context shifts — Thread testimony is no longer heretical, just difficult. Vaynard benefits from Lenneth's programme even though he disagrees with her method.

## 2.5 Lenneth's Archive Development — Threadwork Analysis

See Part One §1.5 for mechanics. The key arc emergence:

**TS 8 (game start):** Non-practitioner. Cannot perceive Thread operations. Her Einhir revival programme is cultural, not ontological. She advocates for Einhir heritage because she believes it is just, not because she has experienced Thread reality.

**TS 20 (mid-game, ~Season 6):** Dormant. Can sense Thread-touched objects. The archive materials she has been handling for years become perceptually different — she begins to notice that certain texts feel significant in ways she cannot articulate. This is Klapp's trajectory applied to a political actor rather than a scholar-priest. The difference: Klapp's institution condemns what he is becoming. Lenneth's programme embraces it.

**TS 30 (late-game, ~Season 11+ or accelerated via breakthrough):** Practitioner threshold. Lenneth can Leap. She can perceive Thread operations. She can understand what Vaynard has been fighting for. The institutional revivalist becomes an ontological witness. Her Belief 2 — "Thread work is not heresy. It is what was here before the Church arrived." — shifts from intellectual conviction to lived experience.

**Arc emergence at each threshold:**

At TS 20, Lenneth gains the Crown's first direct Thread intelligence. She can evaluate Thread-touched objects in the archive, identify which ones are operationally significant, and direct Crown resources toward Thread-relevant goals. Crown gains +1D on Investigate Thread orders.

At TS 30, Lenneth can contact Edeyja directly (Warden Cooperation track becomes accessible to Crown). This is transformative — Crown has been managing the Southernmost as a dead zone to avoid. With Lenneth as bridge to the Wardens, Crown can engage with the actual problem for the first time. The institutional patron has become the institutional bridge.

**Lenneth-Baralta confrontation through threadwork:**
If Lenneth reaches TS 30 and Baralta's Crown claim is active simultaneously, the confrontation gains an ontological dimension the political framework cannot contain. Lenneth can now perceive Thread reality. Baralta cannot. Lenneth's argument for the Crown — "I understand the real threat to this peninsula" — is experientially grounded. Baralta's argument — "I am the most competent governor" — is politically grounded. The deed-logic has no mechanism for evaluating ontological competence against political competence. The system breaks.

## 2.7 The Caste System as Thread Sensitivity Gradient — Arc Emergence

The TS distribution follows the caste gradient:
- South = higher TS, lower Church penetration, greater Forgetting resistance
- North = lower TS, higher Church penetration, complete Forgetting vulnerability

**This creates a structural irony across every arc:**

The post-war settlement suppresses the populations that can perceive the peninsula's existential threat. The settlement's enforcement mechanism (Church, essentialist theology) is the thing that prevents the population from understanding why the settlement must eventually be revised. The caste system is simultaneously the peninsula's political stability mechanism and its epistemological self-destruction mechanism.

**Thread operations are concentrated in the south** — where the TS-capable population lives, where the Church's reach is weakest, where the Restoration Movement has its base. Every Thread operation in the south:
1. Costs Rendering Stability (RS) — the substrate degrades
2. Triggers Church attention — Heresy Investigation risk
3. Advances the Restoration Movement's cultural case — Thread work is real, not heretical
4. Weakens the caste's perceptual foundation — the southerners who are stigmatised are the ones performing the work that everyone will eventually need

**The convergence point:** When RS drops into the Fractured band (39-20), non-practitioners begin experiencing rendering failures — "inconsistent memories, déjà vu, objects in wrong places." At this point, the Forgetting's perceptual barrier begins dissolving for everyone. Northern populations who have never experienced Thread reality start noticing that the world is not stable. The caste's perceptual hierarchy — southerners can see it, northerners cannot — begins collapsing.

This is the point where Vaynard's political programme becomes legible to the population, Lenneth's cultural revival gains experiential support, Baralta's theological framework encounters something it cannot accommodate, and Almud's ethical doubt becomes everyone's ethical doubt. The Thread mechanics produce the political crisis. The political crisis is an expression of the Thread mechanics.

**Arc emergence: The Convergence**
No player designs this. It emerges from the interaction of:
- Church Weaving operations (RS drain, per Arc 5)
- Vaynard's Thread investigations (RS drain from intelligence-adjacent operations)
- Lenneth's Cultural Revival Track (softens Church enforcement, increases southern Thread activity, accelerates RS drain)
- Baralta's TC suppression (delays Church confrontation, allows more Weaving seasons, extends RS drain duration)
- The caste system itself (concentrates Thread activity in the south, where RS costs accumulate)

Every faction's rational political actions — including Baralta's TC suppression, which is the most structurally responsible thing anyone is doing — contribute to RS degradation. The settlement's own defenders are accelerating the substrate's collapse. The settlement is self-consuming.

**This is the Einhir Catastrophe replicated politically.** The Einhir over-drew the threads through coordinated institutional action. The post-war settlement over-draws the threads through uncoordinated institutional inaction. The mechanism is different. The trajectory is identical.

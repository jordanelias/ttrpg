# Valoria — Comprehensive NPC Characterization Audit
## Date: 2026-04-09 | Scope: All 24 named NPCs (13 roster + 11 existing)
## Methodology: Quantitative mention analysis, interaction network mapping, cliché detection, qualitative assessment across 8 criteria

---

## AUDIT CRITERIA

Each NPC is evaluated on:

1. **Complexity** — Does the character contain genuine internal contradiction that produces emergent behaviour, or is the contradiction decorative?
2. **Cliché** — Does the characterization rely on stock types, or does it subvert/transcend them?
3. **Emergence** — Do the character's consequences arise from systemic interaction with game mechanics, or are they scripted plot beats?
4. **Arc Interest** — Is the arc trajectory surprising, branching, and player-dependent, or is it predetermined?
5. **Importance** — Does removing this NPC collapse a mechanical system, or can they be cut without structural loss?
6. **Precedent Quality** — Is the historical echo structural (the parallel illuminates the character's function) or decorative (the parallel sounds impressive)?
7. **Cross-NPC Interaction** — How many other NPCs does this character mechanically interact with? Does any interaction produce emergent consequences?
8. **Mode Coverage** — Does the NPC work across TTRPG/Hybrid/BG, or are they mode-locked?

Scores: 1 (weak) to 5 (exceptional). Total possible: 40.

## CRITICAL FINDINGS

### 1. Mode Coverage Gaps (highest priority)

**Almud** has no BG mechanical expression — no Event Card, no Modifier. The peninsula's central political figure is invisible in board game mode. This is the audit's most significant finding.

**Lenneth** has no Hybrid or BG mechanical expression at all. Zero measurable game-state effects. She is the roster's biggest gap between narrative importance and mechanical presence.

**Baralta** has no BG Event Card despite being a Tier 2 political figure.

### 2. Under-characterised Major NPCs

**Elske** has mechanical impact (Loyalty track) but zero characterization — no conviction, no flaw, no ethics, no echo. She is a mechanism, not a person.

**Torben** is explicitly identified as "a political object" with no personal characterization. Whether this is a design choice or a gap should be stated.

**Himlensendt** and **Olafsson** lack historical echoes.

### 3. Missing Cross-NPC Interactions

**Haelgrund ↔ Torsvald:** Both have hidden TS in institutions that would reject them. This parallel is the most valuable unwritten NPC interaction.

**Solberg ↔ Strand:** Both are "outsiders" in their courts (foreign posting vs conditional appointment). The displacement parallel could produce an interesting cross-faction scene.

**Vaynard ↔ Laskaris:** Varfell intelligence should be monitoring the Altonian representative. No interaction exists.

**Prudence ↔ Olafsson:** Two Cardinals whose portfolios (Economics, Justice) create the most Church-internal friction. No interaction exists.

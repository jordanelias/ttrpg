## B5 — PLAYABLE FACTION CARDS

All rolls: pool = relevant faction stat (1–7). Dice: d6. Success = 4+. Majority 1s = Failure regardless of other dice.

**Ethical framework modifiers** apply before rolling:
- Crown (Virtue Ethics): public, visible actions −1 Ob. Covert or morally ambiguous: +1 Ob.
- Church (Divine Command): doctrine-aligned −1 Ob. Thread-supporting: +2 Ob.
- Hafenmark (Categorical Imperative): procedurally grounded, precedent-following −1 Ob. Ad hoc or precedent-breaking: +1 Ob.
- Varfell (Epistemic Reason): evidence-based Intel −1 Ob. Emotional or reactive: +1 Ob.
- Restoration Movement (Rawlsian): community-benefiting −1 Ob. Hierarchical or exclusionary: +1 Ob.
- Löwenritter (Military Honor): orders protecting Valorian civilians or sovereignty −1 Ob. Orders advancing personal or factional gain at Valoria's expense: +2 Ob.

### FACTION: THE CROWN

**Stats:** Mandate 5 · Influence 5 · Wealth 4 · Military 4 · Stability 4  
**Ethical Framework:** Virtue Ethics  
**Leader:** King Almud Almqvist  
**Conviction (BG-E-59 approved):** "The state is the only legitimate vessel of order."  
**Political Decision Style:** Precedent

**Institutional Mandate:** "The monarchy provides the order that protects Valoria — and all other institutions serve the monarchy's order."

**Mandate trigger events and Compromise benefits (MP-34 approved):**

| Trigger | Compromise Benefit |
|---------|-------------------|
| Church refuses Crown directive in Crown territory | Issue one additional Policy this season without Mandate ≥ 4 requirement |
| Varfell operates Intel in Crown territory without Crown awareness | Crown receives the same information Varfell gathered (shared Intel result) |
| Hafenmark Parliamentary ruling contradicts Crown decree this season | Crown Policy overrides the Parliamentary ruling for 1 season |
| Any faction allies with Altonia (Open Pledge) without Crown prior approval | Crown may join the alliance as a third party at no Standing cost |

**Uphold benefit (all triggers):** +1 Renown on Champion (Almud) + 1 Stability. No mechanical benefit from the triggering situation.

**Unique Power — Royal Decree** (2-season cooldown): Roll Mandate vs. Ob 2. Success: one faction attribute change (any faction, ±1) takes effect immediately. Failure: Crown Mandate −1. Effect declared before rolling.

**Crown-Exclusive — Policy Instruments:** Once per season (in addition to normal card plays), Crown may issue one Policy if Mandate ≥ 4. Same policy cannot repeat for 2 seasons.

| Policy | Effect | Downside |
|--------|--------|----------|
| Royal Taxation | All Trade this season: +1 Wealth to Crown | Non-Crown Trade Ob +1 |
| Conscription Mandate | All Muster: −1 Ob this season | Mustered units begin Cohesion −1 |
| Free Trade Decree | Invasion Pressure (IP) −1; Schoenland Trade +1D | Theocratic Clock (TC) +1 |
| Curfew | Intel/Covert in Crown territories: +2 Ob | Niflhel Standing +2 |
| Parliamentary Session | All factions: one additional Diplomacy this season | Crown Mandate check Ob 1 on failure; Theological Dissatisfaction (TD) −1 |
| Emergency Powers | Crown executes one order face-down (revealed at resolution) | Hafenmark free Parliamentary Manoeuvre; Theocratic Clock (TC) +1; Theological Dissatisfaction (TD) +1 |
| Supremacy Decree (once/game) | Named faction treats Crown Mandate as +2 higher for all opposed rolls this season | All other factions: Standing +1 vs Crown |

**Torben Loyalty Track (0–7, starts at 3):** Tracked on Crown faction mat or shared ledger; visible to all players.

**Gaining Torben Loyalty:**
- Crown Senator Outward (Diplomacy) targeting Torben's location (T1 default): Overwhelming +2; Success +1.
- Crown holds Parliament Integrity (PI) ≥ 5 at Year-End: +1 (Torben respects constitutional governance).
- Crown upholds Institutional Mandate 2+ consecutive seasons: +1 per Year-End it continues.

**Losing Torben Loyalty:**
- Crown Compromises Institutional Mandate: −1.
- Crown issues Emergency Powers: −1.
- Löwenritter Coup fires: Loyalty resets to 1.
- TC crosses 60: −1 (Torben grows concerned about Church dominance under Crown watch).

**Torben's location:** Starts at T1 (Valorsplatz). If T1 changes control: moves to nearest Crown-controlled territory. If no Crown territory exists: Torben is in exile (Loyalty cannot advance until Crown reclaims at least one territory).

**Victory — Constitutional Stability (Primary):** All 5 Deed Tokens held simultaneously + Parliament Integrity (PI) ≥ 3 at Accounting.

| Deed | Condition |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | Mandate ≥ 5 |
| 2 | Control Valorsplatz (T1) + Gransol (T2) + ≥ 2 other territories |
| 3 | Theocratic Clock (TC) < 60 and Invasion Pressure (IP) < 75 simultaneously |
| 4 | Parliament Integrity (PI) ≥ 5 |
| 5 | Torben Loyalty ≥ 5 |

**Victory — Dominion (Alternate):** Controls ≥ 8 territories AND one Submission Condition met simultaneously at Accounting.

| Submission Condition | How Achieved | Standing Cost |
|---------------------|-------------|---------------|
| Church recognizes Crown supremacy over religious appointments in Crown territories | Open Pledge accepted, OR Theocratic Clock (TC) = 0 | +2 Standing vs Crown from Church; Baralta Mandate trigger |
| Varfell formally subordinates Intel to Crown oversight | Varfell Thread Mastery (VTM) ≤ 1 OR Crown captures Vaynard + all Varfell territories | +1 Standing vs Crown from Varfell |
| Hafenmark surrenders Parliamentary independence | Parliament Integrity (PI) = 0 OR Baralta Compromise count ≥ 4 | +2 Standing vs Crown from Hafenmark; Riskbreakers Priority 2 fires against Crown |

### FACTION: THE CHURCH OF SOLMUND

**Stats:** Mandate 5 · Influence 6 · Wealth 5 · Military 4 · Stability 5  
**Ethical Framework:** Divine Command  
**Leader:** Confessor Arne Himlensendt  
**Conviction:** Faith  
**Political Decision Style:** Evidence

**Institutional Mandate:** "Solmund's doctrine is the rightful framework for all governance — secular authority is derivative and conditional."

**Mandate trigger events and Compromise benefits (MP-34 approved):**

| Trigger | Compromise Benefit |
|---------|-------------------|
| Reformed Settlement declared by Hafenmark (Church must respond) | See Reformed Settlement response rules |
| Crown refuses to enforce a Church Interdict in Crown territory | Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) +1 (Church aligns with Altonian support) |
| Heresy Investigation fails (target escapes without penalty) | Uphold only: Himlensendt Renown +1; Compromise: no benefit available |
| Any faction occupies Himmelenger without Church military response | Church calls Crusade: Templar units deploy without Muster; Theocratic Clock (TC) +2, Attention Pool +3 |

**Thread Operations and Klapp:** The Church does not perform Thread operations and does not have access to the Senate Market Pontifex card. The Church's Pontifex card (base hand) is Ecclesiastical Suppression — a Social/Mandate card, not a Thread operation (see below). If a Klapp event occurs (authored text pending — see B13 Klapp note), limited Thread operations become temporarily available but directly undermine Theocratic Clock (TC) accumulation and trigger Inquisitor responses. [BG-E-NEW-03: Klapp event card text required.]

**Church-Exclusive — Pontifex (Ecclesiastical Suppression, base hand card):**  
This is NOT a Thread operation. It is a Social/Mandate action representing the Church's doctrinal authority over Thread-adjacent phenomena.
- **Inward (Doctrinal Inquiry):** Roll Mandate vs. Ob 2 (−1 Ob in Church territory). Open one Heresy Investigation without spending Attention Pool threshold (costs 1 Wealth instead). The Investigation targets one faction's activity in the chosen territory.
- **Outward (Thread Suppression):** If a Thread operation by another faction was resolved this Phase 4 in this territory, roll Mandate vs. Ob 2. Overwhelming: Co-Movement card effect nullified (Rendering Stability (RS) change cancelled); the Thread operation occurred but the Church intercepted its consequences. Success: Actualized dimension (RS effect) of the Co-Movement card is reduced by 1 (minimum 0). Partial: no suppression; Church Attention Pool +1 (the attempt was visible). Failure: No suppression; Church Mandate −1 (failed to act in Solmund's name).

The ethical framework "+2 Ob" penalty for Thread-supporting actions applies to factions other than the Church attempting Thread operations in Church-controlled territories — it does not apply to the Church's own Pontifex (Ecclesiastical Suppression), which is a doctrine-aligned action (−1 Ob instead).

**Church-Exclusive — Theocratic Clock (TC) as Resource:** Theocratic Clock (TC) is simultaneously the Church's victory track and spending resource. Declared before rolling: −1 Theocratic Clock (TC) spent → +1D to any Church action. Maximum 2 Theocratic Clock (TC) per roll. Spending is voluntary and strategic: it reduces the victory threshold simultaneously.

**Theocratic Clock (TC) Advancement Formula:**  
Theocratic Clock (TC) advances each season from the following sources (apply at Seasonal Accounting Step 4):

| Source | TC Gain |
|--------|---------|
| Church controls Himmelenger (T3) | +1/season |
| Assert choice (TC > 50 mandatory) | +1 |
| Church Attention Pool reaches threshold 5 | +1 |
| Emergency Powers policy (Crown or Löwenritter) | +1 |
| Free Trade Decree policy (Crown) | +1 |
| Church unit present in any non-Church territory at season end | +0.5/unit (max +1/season; applies as whole number at Year-End) |
| Church Territorial Seizure (TC ≥ 80, seizes territory) | +2 |
| Reformed Settlement — Resist response | +3 |
| Heresy Investigation confirmed success (target penalized) | +0.5 (accumulates; applies as whole number at Year-End) |
| Doctrinal Reach Milestone (after trigger) | +0.5/season (applies as whole number at Year-End) |

**Theocratic Clock (TC) decreases:**
- Reformed Settlement — Accommodate: −5
- Sovereign Authority Doctrine (Hafenmark, once/game): −2 to −3
- Baralta passive suppression (Mandate ≥ 4): −1/season
- Suppress choice (TC > 50): +0 (no loss, no gain for the season's mandatory Assert/Suppress)
- Löwenritter Requisition Order (success in Church territory): −1
- Royal Decree (Crown, targeting Church TC): −1

**Church-Exclusive — Mandatory Theocratic Assert/Suppress:** Each season Theocratic Clock (TC) > 50, Church must choose:
- **Assert:** Theocratic Clock (TC) +1. All secular faction Mandate triggers fire. Riskbreakers evaluate Priority 3.
- **Suppress:** Theocratic Clock (TC) +0 (no Assert gain). Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) −1. Himlensendt Renown −1.

No neutral option. Announced at Phase 5 Accounting Step 5.

**Church-Exclusive — Inquisitors:** Unit type deployable via Senator Inward with Church flag. While deployed in a territory: +2 Attention Pool per season present. Can open Heresy Investigations without additional card play. Moving an Inquisitor requires a Senator card. Inquisitors withdraw if territory is attacked; they are not combat units.

**Unique Power — Excommunication** (3-season cooldown): Roll Mandate vs. target leader's Mandate (or Ob 2 for non-leaders). Overwhelming: target Mandate −1; barred from Church territories 1 season; Reputation −1 all factions. Success: Mandate −1 only. Failure: Church Mandate −1; target Mandate +1 (martyr sympathy).

**Victory — Holy State (Primary):** Theocratic Clock (TC) ≥ 70 + Mandate ≥ 5 + control Himmelenger + control Valorsplatz + Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) ≥ 3.

| Deed | Condition |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | Theocratic Clock (TC) ≥ 40 |
| 2 | Church Mandate ≥ 5 |
| 3 | Control Himmelenger continuously for 2 seasons |
| 4 | Control Valorsplatz |
| (Gate) | Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) ≥ 3 (not a Deed; required to declare victory) |

**Victory — Dual Theocracy (Alternate):** Theocratic Clock (TC) ≥ 60 + Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) = 5 + Invasion Pressure (IP) ≤ 30. Theological governance through diplomatic mastery. *Note: Invasion Pressure (IP) ≤ 30 requires active reduction (Free Trade Decree, Restoration Network project) — the Church has no direct Invasion Pressure (IP) reduction mechanism. This path requires diplomatic coordination with Crown or Restoration Movement.*

**Hafenmark-Exclusive — Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT, 0–6):** Measures spread of Reformed Doctrine (direct access to Solmund, without Church mediation).

**Gaining Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT):**
- Senator card (Outward) in non-Church territory: Ob 2. Success: +1.
- Theocratic Clock (TC) rises above 50 AND Baralta has a unit in that territory: +1.
- Crown issues Emergency Powers without Parliamentary check: +1.
- Any faction publicly Compromises their Institutional Mandate: +1.

**Losing Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT):**
- Church Excommunicates Baralta: −2.
- Baralta publicly Compromises her own Mandate: −2.
- Rendering Stability (RS) drops below 30: −1/season.

**Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT) Effects:**

| RDT | Effect |
|-----|--------|
| 0–1 | No mechanical effect |
| 2 | Theocratic Clock (TC) gains this season halved (rounded down) |

| 4 | Theocratic Clock (TC) capped at current level + 5 for remainder of game |
| 5 | Reformed Settlement available (once/game) |
| 6 | All Diplomatic actions targeting Hafenmark: +1 Ob |

**Reformed Settlement Declaration:** When Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT) reaches 5, Hafenmark may declare Reformed Settlement during **Phase 2 (Negotiation)** as a free action — no card play required. Church responds at Phase 5 Accounting Step 5. Other factions may negotiate around the response during Phase 2.

**Reformed Settlement (RDT 5, once/game):** Baralta declares the theological right of direct faithful access. Church must respond:
- **Resist:** Theocratic Clock (TC) +3; Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT) +1 (martyrdom); Hafenmark +1 Standing vs Church, +1 Deed Token.
- **Accommodate:** Theocratic Clock (TC) −5; Church Mandate trigger; Church Stability −1; Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) −2.
- **Ignore:** Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT) +1 next season. **Maximum 2 uses of Ignore.** On the third occurrence, Church must Resist or Accommodate.

**Hafenmark-Exclusive — Theological Dissatisfaction (TD, 0–5, private):** Tracked privately on faction mat. Other players observe effects, not the score.

**Gaining Theological Dissatisfaction (TD):** Crown issues Emergency Powers (+1); Crown submits to Altonian pressure without resistance (+1); Crown fails to respond to Church incursion in Crown territory (+1).  
**Losing Theological Dissatisfaction (TD):** Almud issues Parliamentary Session (−1); Crown collaborates with Hafenmark on policy (−1).

**Theological Dissatisfaction (TD) Effects:**

| TD | Effect |
|----|--------|
| 0–2 | No effect |
| 3 | Hafenmark +1D Diplomatic actions targeting Crown |
| 4 | Baralta may declare Almud theologically unworthy publicly: Crown −1 Deed Token; Hafenmark +1 Standing vs Crown |
| 5 | Rival Succession Claim: Hafenmark +1D all orders in Crown-adjacent territories; no Standing penalty for seizing Crown territories if Civil War fires |

**Unique Power — Sovereign Authority Doctrine (once/game):** Hafenvalor only. Roll Mandate vs. Ob 4. Overwhelming: Theocratic Clock (TC) −3, Church Mandate −1, Heresy Investigation blocked this season. Success: Theocratic Clock (TC) −2, Church Mandate −1, Investigation opens against Baralta. Failure: Theocratic Clock (TC) +1, Investigation fires, Baralta Mandate −1.

**Theocratic Clock (TC) Passive Suppression:** While Baralta's Mandate ≥ 4: Theocratic Clock (TC) −1/season. Pauses if Mandate drops below 4.

**Deed Token Conditions:**

| 2 | Theological Dissatisfaction (TD) = 5 |

*Path C — Parliamentary Consolidation:* 4 Deed Tokens required.

### FACTION: LÖWENRITTER (post-coup only)

**Activation:** The Löwenritter begin as NPC-controlled (partial sheet, see B13). If Coup Counter reaches 4 and coup fires successfully (Military vs. Ob 3), Löwenritter gains this full sheet. In a 4-player game they remain NPC-controlled even post-coup. In solo play, Löwenritter post-coup is a viable solo faction (see B16).

**Stats (post-coup):** Mandate 3 · Influence 2 · Wealth 3 · Military 6 · Stability 5  
**Ethical Framework:** Military Honor  
**Leader:** Grandmaster Sigrid Ehrenwall (Late 50s)  
**Conviction:** Valoria endures, whatever the cost.

**Institutional Mandate:** "The Löwenritter exist to protect Valoria when no one else will. We hold until Valoria can govern itself."

**Mandate trigger events and Compromise benefits (MP-34 approved):**

| Trigger | Compromise Benefit |
|---------|-------------------|
| Church seizes Löwenritter-governed territory without military response | Cede territory diplomatically; Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) +1 |
| Parliament Integrity (PI) drops to 0 during Löwenritter rule | Issue declaration of permanent emergency governance; no Parliament Integrity (PI) penalties for 2 seasons |
| Emergency Powers issued more than twice in one year | Third Emergency Powers use is legal; no Standing cost; Theocratic Clock (TC) +2 |
| Invasion Pressure (IP) crosses 75 without a military order that season | Cede T4 (Spartfell) to Altonian Vanguard as buffer zone; Invasion Pressure (IP) halted for 2 seasons |

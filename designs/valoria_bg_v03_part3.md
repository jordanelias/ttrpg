## B6 — THREAD SYSTEMS

### Thread Resonance

Thread Resonance is a binary seasonal marker. A faction is either **in Thread Resonance** or **not in Thread Resonance** this season. The marker is held on the faction mat and cleared at Accounting Step 7.

**Gaining Thread Resonance this season:**
- A Thread operation occurs in or adjacent to a faction's controlled territory.
- A Co-Movement card effect targets the faction.
- Rendering Stability (RS) drops below a threshold this season (all factions gain Thread Resonance).
- Restoration Movement: also gains Thread Resonance in any territory with a Presence marker.
- Varfell Varfell Thread Mastery (VTM) ≥ 2: always in Thread Resonance in T9. Always qualifies for Thread-active territory checks.

**While in Thread Resonance:**
- +1D to Intel orders targeting practitioners or Thread-active sites.
- Senate Market purchase of Pontifex card: −1 cost.
- Faction qualifies for Thread-qualified presence test for T12/T13 (combined with appropriate card play).

**Thread Resonance for qualified factions only** (Restoration Movement, Varfell Varfell Thread Mastery (VTM) ≥ 2, or faction with Thread Sensitivity 30+ agent in hybrid mode):
- May ask one yes/no question about Rendering Stability (RS) (above or below a threshold) this season.
- May spend Thread Resonance marker to: cancel one Co-Movement card effect targeting them, OR contribute +1D to Restoration Community Weaving in their territory.

**Thread Witness Nodes:** When a Community Weave Project is completed, the territory gains a permanent Thread Witness Node marker. Any Thread operation in this territory draws one additional Co-Movement card.

### Thread Debt

**"Against temporal flow" defined:** A Thread operation is against temporal flow when any of the following apply at the time of the operation:
- The territory already contains one or more Thread Debt tokens from a prior operation (strained substrate).
- Rendering Stability (RS) is below 30 at the time the operation is declared.
- The Varfell Varfell Thread Mastery (VTM) 5 ability is used (always produces Thread Debt regardless of other conditions).

Standard Restoration Community Weaving and standard Pontifex (Senate Market) Thread operations do **not** inherently incur Thread Debt unless one of the above conditions applies.

**Thread Debt tokens:**
- Active drain: Rendering Stability (RS) −1 per outstanding unserviced token per season (Accounting Step 6).
- Service: faction executes a Mend-equivalent action (Restoration Weaver card in same territory, or spend Thread Resonance marker while qualified). Serviced token: active drain ceases. Permanent residual Rendering Stability (RS) −0.5 recorded on faction ledger.
- Maximum 3 Thread Debt tokens simultaneously. At 3: no further Debt incurrence until reduced to 2.
- Church may use outstanding Thread Debt as grounds for automatic Heresy Investigation: Ob −1 if target faction holds ≥ 2 tokens.
- Thread Debt incurred by any faction: Varfell Thread Mastery (VTM) +1 for Varfell (Vaynard perceives the substrate strain).

**Year-End Thread Debt resolution:** All residual Rendering Stability (RS) fractions sum to whole number (round up) and apply to Rendering Stability (RS) track at Year-End Accounting.

### Church Attention Pool

Tracks social indicators of Thread and heretical activity. The Church detects consequences, not operations.

**Pool accumulates from:**
- Unauthorized community gathering reported in any territory: +1 Attention.
- Unexplained structural/social change in territory (Thread operation visible effects): +2 Attention.
- Einhir artefact surfaces (Event Card C-13): +2 Attention.
- Restoration community gathering in Church-adjacent territory: +3 Attention.
- Faction behaviour consistent with heretical influence in multiple territories: +1 per territory showing anomaly.
- Löwenritter Requisition Order (success or partial): +1–2 Attention (see B5 Löwenritter).
- Warden investigation fires in T13 (Edeyja AI): +1 Attention.

**Attention thresholds (pool resets each season at Accounting Step 5):**

| Pool | Response |
|------|----------|
| 3 | Church opens one Heresy Investigation (free, no card play) |
| 5 | Theocratic Clock (TC) +1 |
| 7 | Inquisitor protocol: all Thread-active factions −1D to covert/Intel this season. Inquisition Autonomy fires. (Modified to 8 if Reformed Doctrine Track (RDT) ≥ 3.) |
| 10 | Church Crusade: Templar units may deploy anywhere in Church territory without Muster card |

**Thread Operation Procedure (print and laminate):**
1. Declare card (Weaver or Senate Market Pontifex) and orientation.
2. Declare territory and Thread operation type.
3. Check "against temporal flow" conditions. If any apply: incur Thread Debt token (place in territory, −1 Ob to this operation as substrate resistance).
4. Apply Ob modifiers: Rendering Stability (RS) environmental, Presence markers, Thread Debt already present, Champion.
5. Roll. Determine degree.
6. Apply result.
7. Draw Co-Movement card (+ 1 additional card if territory has Thread Witness Node). Apply all three-dimensional effects.
8. Check Attention Pool: do visible consequences add Attention? Apply.

**Niflhel Exposure and Rendering Stability (RS) Stabilization:** To fully stabilize Rendering Stability (RS) decline, Niflhel Network Depth in active territories must be reduced to 0. Execute via successful Intel action (Tribune Investigate, Ob 4) or Warden investigation (automatic if Niflhel detected in T13 with Warden Cooperation ≥ 1). Warden-Restoration cooperation arrests the feedback loop; Niflhel exposure reverses it.

### Co-Movement Cards (P-14 compliance)

Every Thread operation draws one Co-Movement card. Thread Witness Node territories draw two (apply both). Cards cannot be declined. Cards produce consequences across three axes:
1. **Actualized dimension:** Rendering Stability (RS) change.
2. **Epistemic dimension:** Information revealed, concealed, or distorted.
3. **Temporal dimension:** History/continuity marker — Project disruption, Pledge complication, or NPC relationship change.

20-card base deck + GT-02 additions (approved). Co-Movement cards can be cancelled only by a qualified faction spending its Thread Resonance marker (one cancellation per marker spent).

## B7 — POLITICAL SYSTEMS

### Institutional Mandate — Uphold / Compromise

Each faction has a stated Institutional Mandate. When a trigger event occurs, the faction must respond:

- **Uphold:** Act consistently with Mandate, even at cost. Earns 1 Renown on Champion + 1 Stability. No mechanical benefit from the triggering situation.
- **Compromise:** Act against Mandate for strategic advantage. Earns the Compromise benefit specific to that trigger (see B5 per faction). Costs: 1 Standing token (visible to others) + 1 Stability.

In hybrid mode: if PC faction leader's personal Belief arc contradicts the faction's Uphold/Compromise pattern, Hollow Victory condition activates.

### Standing Tokens

**Gaining Standing against another faction:**
- That faction breaks an Open Pledge in your favour.
- That faction executes Brutal disposition against Valorian civilians.
- That faction seizes your territory while you had an active non-broken Pledge.
- Church Excommunicates your faction leader.
- That faction Compromises their Institutional Mandate publicly.
- That faction's action directly targeted you this season.

**Spending Standing:**

| Cost | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 1 Standing | +1D to any one defensive roll this season |
| 2 Standing | Execute one defensive order at Ob −1 |
| 3 Standing | Respond to an action targeting you (out-of-priority defensive card play) |
| 4 Standing | Convert to 1 Wealth, 1 Mandate, or 1 Influence |

3+ Standing against one faction from you: their Diplomacy targeting you automatically fails. 5+: they cannot ally with you or give you Deal Tokens.

## B11 — YEAR-END ACCOUNTING

Fires every 4th season (Winter).

1. **Annual Attrition:** Each unit that fought ≥ 1 battle this year: Cohesion check Ob 1. Failure: Cohesion −1.
2. **Sustenance:** Each military unit costs 1 Prosperity from controlling territory per year. Territories below Prosperity 2: excess units disbanded.
3. **Thread Debt residuals:** Sum all residual Rendering Stability (RS) fractions (0.5 per serviced Thread Debt token across game) → apply as whole-number Rendering Stability (RS) loss (round up).
4. **Niflhel Rendering Stability (RS) passive drain (NPC accounting):** −0.5 per Niflhel-operated territory (Network Depth ≥ 1) → apply as whole numbers (round up). *To eliminate this drain: expose Niflhel Network Depth via Intel action (Ob 4) or Warden investigation.*
5. **Thread Wound territories:** T12/T13 occupied ≥ 2 consecutive seasons by any faction: Rendering Stability (RS) −1 per additional season. Warden Cooperation ≥ 1 reduces this: Rendering Stability (RS) −0.5 instead.
6. **Doctrinal Reach accumulation:** If Church has met the Doctrinal Reach Milestone, apply accumulated +0.5 Theocratic Clock (TC)/season to Theocratic Clock (TC) now (as whole number, round down).
7. **Stability Recovery:** Each faction that did not engage in military action this year AND has no active Heresy Investigation: Stability +1.
8. **Pledge accounting:** Any Open Pledges not honored: automatically exposed. Betrayed faction: Standing +2 vs. betrayer.
9. **Milestone Bonuses (Year-End):** Confirm all Milestone triggers that fired this year. Apply any outstanding.
10. **Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) adjustment:** Apply all end-of-year Altonian Ecclesiastical Relationship (AER) changes.
11. **Löwenritter check (if post-coup active):** Is Martial Law still in effect? If Parliament Integrity (PI) = 0 and Military Consolidation is not yet eligible: Löwenritter Mandate −1. Check Torben constitutional restraint Loyalty advancement: did Löwenritter maintain Parliament Integrity (PI) ≥ 3 AND issue no Emergency Powers this year? If yes: Torben Loyalty +1.
12. **Season Objective reveal** (at seasons 4, 8, 12): flip face-down Season Objective token; apply scoring bonus this season.

## B15 — COOPERATIVE VICTORY

Two players may declare a co-victory if their winning conditions are not directly contradictory. Co-victory does not require formal alliance — it requires structural interest alignment. Players declare their co-victory intent publicly during Phase 2 (Negotiation) in any season; from that point, both victory paths are evaluated together at Accounting Step 12.

A co-victory is achieved when both factions hold all required Deed Tokens simultaneously at the same Accounting.

**Approved co-victory pairings:**

| Pair | Path Name | Requirements |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Crown + Hafenmark | Constitutional Co-Victory | Crown holds Constitutional Stability conditions AND Hafenmark holds Reformed Valoria (Path A) conditions simultaneously |
| Crown + Varfell | Informed Sovereignty | Crown holds Constitutional Stability AND Varfell holds Intelligence Hegemony simultaneously. *Note: Varfell Intel in Crown territory does not trigger Crown Mandate during an active co-victory declaration between these factions.* |
| Varfell + Restoration | Substrate Preservation | Varfell holds Thread Supremacy (Path C) conditions AND Restoration holds Network victory conditions simultaneously. Both require Rendering Stability (RS) ≥ 50. |
| Hafenmark + Restoration | Reformed Commonwealth | Hafenmark holds Reformed Valoria (Path A) conditions AND Restoration holds Network victory conditions simultaneously |
| Löwenritter + Hafenmark | Regency Alliance | Löwenritter holds Regency Resolution conditions AND Hafenmark holds Parliamentary Consolidation (Path C) conditions simultaneously |

**Co-victory Hollow Victory:** Both players' Hollow Victory modifiers apply to the co-victory declaration. If either player's effective Deed count falls below the required threshold after Hollow modifiers, the co-victory is void (but the higher-count player may still claim solo victory if eligible).

**Pairings that cannot co-victory (directly contradictory conditions):**
- Church and Hafenmark: Theocratic Clock (TC) ≥ 70 (Church Holy State) vs. Theocratic Clock (TC) suppressed (Hafenmark paths).
- Crown and Church: Control Valorsplatz is required by both Holy State and Constitutional Stability.
- Crown and Löwenritter: Constitutional Stability requires Parliament Integrity (PI) ≥ 5 and Torben Loyalty ≥ 5 — conditions the coup directly undermines.

## B16 — SOLO MODE

Solo Mode is available for all playable factions (see A.3). The solo player manages their faction normally. All other factions run on NPC AI priority trees (B13).

### Solo Setup

1. Select a faction. Set up board normally per faction starting positions.
2. Assign all NPC factions their AI priority tree. Place all NPC Champions in starting positions.
3. Determine NPC play order for Phase 4 Resolution: Stability order (descending), as written in their AI blocks.

### Phase 2 (Negotiation) — Solo Rules

Phase 2 becomes Strategic Planning. No negotiation occurs between player and NPCs. The player may:
- Purchase from the Senate Market at standard cost.
- Declare Open Pledges with NPC factions (the NPC will honor mechanically consistent terms — see below).
- Review all public board state.

**NPC Pledge Compliance:** A player may offer an Open Pledge to an NPC faction by spending 1 Wealth to draft terms. The NPC honors the pledge if compliance is consistent with its current AI priority tree. The player announces the pledge; if the NPC's highest active priority does not conflict with the pledge terms: it is honored automatically. If the pledge conflicts with the NPC's Priority 1 or 2: the NPC declines (no cost to either party).

### Phase 4 (Resolution) — Solo NPC Actions

After the player's cards are revealed (Phase 3), execute NPC card plays in Stability order:
- Each NPC plays one card per season consistent with its AI priority tree. In complex situations: run the highest applicable priority.
- NPC cards are treated as face-down during Phase 1 and revealed simultaneously with the player's cards at Phase 3.
- NPCs do not exceed 5 cards per season.

### Solo Victory Conditions

Victory conditions are unchanged. Apply Hollow Victory modifiers normally. The solo player's win probability is calibrated for a 12–20 season campaign. Session victories (3–5 seasons) may not be achievable on all paths in solo — evaluate progress as partial wins: "Season 5 state: 3 of 5 Deed Tokens held" is a meaningful result.

### Solo Difficulty Modifiers

| Difficulty | Modifier |
|------------|---------|
| Standard | NPC factions follow AI priority trees exactly |
| Challenging | Each NPC faction may deviate one priority level higher against the player once per year |
| Hard | Add one additional NPC faction to starting configuration; all NPC Stability +1 |

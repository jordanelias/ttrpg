## 12. The Seam Text

A passage of otherwise orthodox scripture in which the underlying witness testimony breaks through the theological overlay. The seam is comprehensible only to readers with TS 30+. To everyone else, it reads as particularly vivid devotional language.

**Example:**

> And Solmund came to the village where the well had gone dry. And he stood at the well's edge and did not speak and did not move and the water returned. Not as rain returns — from above, falling. The water returned from below. It rose as if the ground remembered holding water and held it again. And the village elder said: "He did not command the water. The water was already there. He only — " and the elder could not finish, because the word he needed was not a word any of us possessed.

Orthodox reading: Solmund miraculously restored the well. Practitioner reading: Structural-scale weaving re-actualising the water-bearing configuration. The elder's incomplete sentence is the seam.

**Mechanical function:** Discoverable POI (Remnant type) in Church territories. The mechanism is a comprehension gate, not a perception gate. The canon reference is P-08 (epistemological barrier, §10.1-10.2), not the visibility table (§2.3). A non-sensitive reader can read every word. They cannot recognise that the description IS describing threadwork because they have no experiential referent. A TS 30+ reader has seen threads. The same words suddenly cohere with experience. "The ground remembered" — they have perceived configurations reasserting prior states. "Returned from below" — they have felt the direction of Mending operations. The second reading is not revealed content. It is the same content, comprehended differently.

**Godot implementation:** The Seam Text is NOT hidden text revealed at a TS threshold. It is the same text with an added annotation layer — a character-internal recognition response. When TS ≥ 30, the player character's reaction displays alongside the text: a thought, a flash of recognition, a connection to something they have experienced. The scripture object itself does not change. The player's relationship to it changes. This is P-08 operating in reverse: previously-inert knowledge becomes active when the reader gains the experiential framework.

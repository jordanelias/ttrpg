## 15. Folk and Vernacular

| Artifact | Register | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Harvest Songs | Levertov | Describe growth, harvest, preservation. No Solmund by name. Matched to physical labour. |
| Threshold Prayers | Di Cicco | Brief formulae at doorways, journey-starts. Faith as reflex. |
| Sailors' Invocations | Mixed | Orthodoxy + superstition. Acknowledge Maritime Forgetting without understanding it. |
| Funerary Rites | Thomas | Death = thread returning to loom. No resurrection. "Thread" and "pattern," not "soul." |

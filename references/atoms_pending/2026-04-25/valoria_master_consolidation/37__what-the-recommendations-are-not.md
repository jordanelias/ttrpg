## What the recommendations are not

- Not comprehensive. Acknowledged gaps in §8.
- Not at uniform specification depth. Recommendation #3 (literal rendering) is near-implementable. Recommendation #1 (Five Moments) requires Jordan's authorial decisions before becoming implementable. Recommendation #4 (chroniclers) specifies voice-level but not prompt-template-level. Different recommendations need different next steps.
- Not numerically calibrated. Specific values (Rendering Strain bands at 5/10/15/20, etc.) are placeholder pending simulation. Calibration is Phase IV work.
- Not validated. Recommendations are coherent with foundations and complementary to existing audit findings, but no simulation validates that any specific recommendation produces the intended effect. Phase IV simulation is the validator.
- Not exhaustive of foundations work. The recommendations close specific identified gaps. Other foundations-compliance work may be required as the design develops; this set isn't final.

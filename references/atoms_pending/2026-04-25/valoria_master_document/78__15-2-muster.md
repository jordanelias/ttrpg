## 15.2 Muster

Size = 2 (base) + Prosperity modifier (0/+1/+2). Power = floor(Military/2)+1. Prerequisites gate by Prosperity, Wealth, officer proficiency. On Muster success + Wealth failure: downgrade to Light Infantry.

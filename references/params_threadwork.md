## Operations [PROPOSAL — threadwork_redesign_v25.md]
Six operations. TN 7 standard; TN 8 for Forced Resolution and Past-Oriented Pulling.
Co-movement fires on EVERY operation (P-01 compliance — all three dimensional auto-effects mandatory):
1. Temporal auto-effect (CD + History Resonance)
2. Epistemic auto-effect (investigation/testimony/Certainty modifiers)
3. Actual auto-effect (d6 consequence table)

Operations: Weave / Mend / Pull (Present/Past/Future) / Lock / Dissolve / Knot

## Coherence (Personal) [PROPOSAL]
Range: 0–10. Starting: GM-assigned per character (typically 7–9).
Coherence 0: TS-gated branching outcomes. Reality-strain cost for high-TS beings.
Coherence cannot exceed 10. Cannot be purchased at CP.

## The Leap [PROPOSAL]
Triggers Thread → Personal scale transition.
Contact duration begins on round Leap succeeds.
Leap auto-fails if TT ≤ 19 (Dormant) or practitioner Coherence = 0.

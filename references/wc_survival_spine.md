## THE TWO CONTESTS

Valoria's victory architecture establishes two simultaneous contests:

1. **The Political Contest:** Who governs the peninsula? Resolved through faction competition — territory control, institutional power, military force, diplomatic leverage.
2. **The Survival Contest:** Does the world survive? Resolved through Rendering Stability management — the peninsula's thread-substrate must be maintained above Rupture.

These contests share resources (time, practitioner attention, faction capacity) but have different win conditions. A faction that wins the political contest while RS reaches 0 loses — Rupture ends the campaign for all factions.

## WC IS THE MECHANISM OF THE SURVIVAL CONTEST

Warden Cooperation (WC 0–3) is the only faction-choice mechanism that directly addresses the survival contest. The RS budget (rs_budget.md) demonstrates:

- At WC 0, peak drain (~23 RS/season) reaches Rupture in under 3 seasons from TTRPG starting RS.
- At WC 2, Gap/Lock drain halved — extends survival by ~2 seasons.
- At WC 3, Edeyja active Mending (+2/season) — the only viable endgame RS path.

**WC 3 is not one of several survival options. It is the singular path.** There is no alternative combination of individual Mending operations, Lock resolution, or Gap closure that sustains RS against peak drain without WC 3's +2/season from active Edeyja Mending. This is by design: the survival contest has one answer — cross-faction cooperation toward Warden support.

## THE IP/EXPEDITION TENSION

High Invasion Pressure (IP) forces military attention exactly when Southernmost expedition maintenance is most needed. This tension is the strategic architecture connecting the two contests:

- IP demands military resources (faction Military stat, unit deployment, territorial defense).
- Southernmost expedition demands practitioner resources (TS 30+ champions, expedition seasons, Warden engagement).
- Both compete for the same faction bandwidth.

A faction optimizing for political victory (military expansion, territorial control) sacrifices WC advancement. A faction optimizing for WC advancement (expedition support, practitioner allocation to Southernmost) sacrifices political position. This is the game's core strategic dilemma.

## VIDEOGAME IMPLEMENTATION

WC must have equal UI real estate to RS. These are the survival contest's two axes:
- **RS:** How damaged is the substrate? (Displayed: world health indicator.)
- **WC:** How much are you doing about it? (Displayed: Warden cooperation indicator.)

Every other track (TC, IP, Stability, Mandate, Strain) is the political contest. WC and RS together are the survival contest. Players must be able to see both at all times to make informed strategic decisions about the two-contest architecture.

## WR AS PREREQUISITE

WC advancement requires Warden Recognition (WR) thresholds:
- WC 1: WR ≥ 2 (Wardens recognise Vaynard as steward)
- WC 2: WR ≥ 2
- WC 3: WR 3 (Active cooperation)

WR advancement requires successful Southernmost Expeditions. The pathway: Expedition → WR → WC → RS recovery. This chain is the mechanical expression of the survival contest.

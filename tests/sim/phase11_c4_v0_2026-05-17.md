## Findings

- F1. M1 (reach gate) dominant; M2/M3 secondary (5-15pp max).
- F2. C4 redistributes dominance: light-vs-light still Agi-favored 94/6; light-vs-heavy now heavy-favored 78/22 (inverted from 99/1).
- F3. F3 gap (light + STR) only partially closed. Mighty-light 88% loss vs Fast.
- F4. Balanced builds non-viable (17.8% vs Fast). Forced specialization is the cost.
- F5. M3 preempt provides modest recovery (Att8 build holds 26.7%, up from prior 5-8%).

## Three Readings

A. Tune M1 softer (2D+History, reach gap cap, faster closing).
B. Reframing 2 working — weapon-class dominance is Fire Emblem triangle.
C. C4 + C2 hybrid — weapon-keyed primary attribute on top of M1.

## Open canonical questions

1. Is M1 magnitude (1D+History) canonical or do we soften?
2. Does Phase 12 (mass-battle archetype) show heavy/Strong dominance at comparable magnitude?
3. C2 layered on M1 — implement and re-test?

## Sim limitations

- Heuristic stance AI; principled stance selection would shift results.
- Binary reach gate (could be graduated).
- M2 counter magnitudes are empirical guesses; tune in follow-on phase.
- Single weapon×armor pair tested.

## PART ONE: CORRECTED THREADWORK-AS-FIELDWORK MODEL

### §1 The Error

The prior simulation (PP-578) asserted: "Lock and Dissolution produce 0 Evidence Track progress."
This is ontologically false. Per the Foundations §1: "Threads weave organic beings and inorganic
matter as well as thoughts, concepts, and memories. All that is experienceable, both internally
and externally, is woven from threads." There is no configuration excluded from Thread operations,
and no Thread operation that cannot produce investigatively relevant consequences.

### §2 Corrected Rule: Any Thread Operation May Advance Investigation

**Every Thread operation produces three-dimensional co-movement (P-01). Any of those consequences
may yield information relevant to an active investigation.** The Evidence Track advances when the
operation's consequences reveal something the investigator did not previously know, regardless of
operation type.

**Evidence progress from Thread operations:**

| Operation | Primary Effect | Investigative Yield | Evidence Progress |
|-----------|---------------|--------------------|--------------------|
| Thread-Read | Perception of Thread configuration | Direct: the practitioner sees what is there | +2 (Success), +3 (Overwhelming) |
| Weaving | Cohering a configuration | Co-movement may reveal adjacent structures; stabilised configs are easier to examine | +1 if co-movement reveals (GM judgment) |
| Pulling (standard) | Opening a configuration | The pulled state reveals what was hidden, frozen, or compressed | +2 (Success), +3 (Overwhelming) |
| Past-Oriented Pulling | Displacing temporal configuration | The displaced past reveals what was overwritten; the orphaned config is evidence itself | +2 (Success), +3 (Overwhelming) |
| Locking | Freezing a configuration | The locked state preserves evidence against tampering or decay; may reveal the configuration's resistance to Lock (structures that resist Lock are themselves informative) | +1 (preservation), +2 if Lock resistance reveals structure |
| Dissolution | Destroying a configuration | The Gap left behind is shaped by what was there — Gap topology IS forensic evidence; concealment Weavings destroyed by Dissolution reveal what they were hiding | +2 if concealment destroyed; +1 from Gap topology (requires Thread-Read follow-up) |
| Mending | Repairing substrate damage | Repairing a Gap reveals the original configuration (the practitioner must perceive the Gap's structure to repair it); Mending a Locked boundary opens access to deeper content | +1 per Mending success (reveals original structure); +2 if Mending opens access |
| Collective | Pooled operation at any type | Same as base type, but at larger scale — may reveal Structural/Foundational information inaccessible to solo ops | Same as base type at achieved scale |

**The investigative yield depends on what is targeted and why, not on the operation type.**
A Dissolution that destroys a Woven concealment over a crime scene advances the investigation
because the destruction revealed what was hidden. A Lock on an unrelated configuration advances
nothing. The GM determines whether the operation's consequences yield investigation-relevant
information. The Evidence Track is not automatically advanced by every Thread operation — only by
those whose co-movement produces new knowledge about the investigation question.

### §3 Scale, Distance, and Breadth

Thread operations have three dimensions of scope that affect investigative reach:

**Scale (stage11 §11.1):** Object → Personal → Relational → Territorial → Structural.
Higher scale = broader investigative reach but higher Ob, higher Coherence cost, higher RS cost.
A Territorial-scale Thread-Read perceives the Thread configuration of an entire settlement's
substrate — vastly more informative than Object-scale, but costs Coherence −1 and requires TS 50+.

**Distance:** Thread operations target configurations the practitioner can contact. Contact range
is limited by the Leap — the practitioner must be present at the site (or have a Knot connection
to the target). Investigation at a distance requires either physical travel to the site or Knot-
mediated contact (threadwork_redesign_v25.md §8).

**Knots as investigative channels:** A practitioner Knotted to an NPC can Thread-Read that NPC's
configuration without being physically present, subject to Knot strain mechanics. This is
investigative surveillance through relational Thread contact. Each use: +1 Knot strain (the
relationship bears force from being used as an intelligence channel).

**Temporal reach (POP only):** POP accesses past configurations. Recency determines Ob.
Same-session (Ob 3) → generational (Ob 7+). Temporal reach is the deepest investigative
capability — it accesses what no longer exists in the present. But it produces the most severe
consequences: RS −3 minimum, temporal auto-effect, orphaned configurations.

**Breadth (Collective operations):** A collective operation pools multiple practitioners' dice,
extending investigative reach to scales and Obs that solo practitioners cannot achieve.
Collective Thread-Read at Territorial scale: the lattice of practitioners perceives the substrate
of an entire territory simultaneously. This is the Einhir's capability in miniature — and it
carries the Einhir's costs (Coherence loss for all participants, RS drain proportional to scale).

### §4 Thread Operations as Forensic Tools

**Dissolution forensics:** When a configuration is Dissolved, the resulting Gap has a topology
that reflects the dissolved structure. A Thread-Read of a fresh Gap (same scene) at Ob 3
(Micro-Gap) reveals the topology — what was there before destruction. This is Thread-forensics:
destroy to reveal. The moral weight is real (P-04: monstrosity is ontological, not moral — but
Dissolution of a sentient being's configuration raises questions about what the practitioner is
willing to destroy for knowledge). The investigative power is also real.

**Lock forensics:** A configuration that resists Lock reveals its internal structure through the
resistance pattern. Attempting to Lock a Thread-concealment and failing tells the practitioner:
"something here is actively maintaining itself against forced resolution." The Failure itself is
information. A successful Lock freezes the configuration for examination — it is now stable and
can be Thread-Read repeatedly without degradation.

**POP forensics:** POP displaces a configuration to its past state. The displaced state and the
current state co-exist briefly in a paradox window (PP-193). During the window, a Thread-Read
perceives BOTH states simultaneously — the past and the present overlaid. This is the deepest
investigative tool available: the practitioner sees what changed and how. After the window closes,
physical facts update to match the displaced state (the pulled past becomes the present). The
paradox window is the investigation's golden moment.

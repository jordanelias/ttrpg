# SIM-X-06: Personal Combat — Ehrenwall (Löwenritter) vs Templar Captain
## Mode: C (Full Scenario) + D (Edge cases: wound -1D under new system, Inquisitor CE trigger)
## Mechanics: §8 Personal Combat × New wound system (−1D) × §13 Inquisitor CE × §13 NPCs
## Context: Ehrenwall confronts a Templar captain (Brother Haelmund) attempting to detain a practitioner-scholar (Maret Uln) in Crown territory. Ehrenwall invokes Löwenritter jurisdiction; Haelmund refuses. Combat.

- Agility: 4, STR: 4, Endurance: 5
- Weapon: Heavy Cut (longsword, Long reach). Hit TN6, Def TN7, Damage +4.
- Armour: Medium. DR vs HeavyCut=3, vs LightCut=4, vs HeavyBlunt=1.
- Combat Pool: (4×2)+3+3 = 14. Stamina: 5+3+1−1(medium) = 8. Health: 5. Max Wounds: 3.
- CR: 5 (for mass battle purposes). Personal conviction: Duty.

**Brother Haelmund (Templar Captain)**
- Agility: 3, STR: 4, Endurance: 4
- Weapon: Heavy Blunt (mace, Short reach). Hit TN7, Def TN8, Damage +4.
- Armour: Heavy. DR vs HeavyCut=5, vs LightCut=6, vs HeavyBlunt=1.
- Combat Pool: (3×2)+2+3 = 11. Stamina: 4+2+1−2(heavy) = 5. Health: 4. Max Wounds: 3.

**Observer: Inquisitor Vald (CE track: 2)**
- Present but not combatant. Will accumulate CE from witnessing combat.

**Range:** Ehrenwall Long (correct range = Far zone). Haelmund Short (correct range = Close zone).
Start: contested. Both can attack only at their preferred zone. Need to establish range first.

## Round 1 — Range Contest

**Ehrenwall:** Establish Distance (wants Far zone where longsword is effective). Roll Offence dice at TN7 vs Haelmund's Offence successes. With initiative, Ehrenwall sees Haelmund's action type before committing.

Haelmund declares: Strike attempt (forcing Close zone, mace range).
Ehrenwall sees Strike, commits to Establish Distance.

**Establish Distance resolution:**
- Ehrenwall splits: all dice to Establish Distance (Offence dice, TN7) vs Haelmund's Offence successes.
- Ehrenwall 14D TN7 (Offence). Expected net: 14×0.33 = 4.6.
- Haelmund Strike Offence (11D split, say 7D Off): 7D TN7 (HeavyBlunt hit TN7). Expected net: 7×0.33 = 2.3.

Establish Distance: success if Ehrenwall's successes ≥ Haelmund's Offence AND Ehrenwall not hit.
Haelmund hits Ehrenwall: P(7D TN7 vs Ehrenwall's Defence — but Ehrenwall committed all dice to Distance, no Defence allocation).

Wait — Establish Distance: "Roll Offence dice at TN7. Succeed if successes ≥ opponent's Offence successes AND not hit."
Ehrenwall commits all dice to the action; 0D defence. Haelmund's Strike with 7D (and no opposition on Defence) hits automatically if Haelmund has any net successes.

**Critical interaction:** Ehrenwall has 0 Defence dice. Haelmund's 7D Strike (TN7): P(≥1 net) ~73%. Most likely Haelmund hits.

Ehrenwall damage from Haelmund hit: Excess successes ~2.3 (all vs 0) + STR4 + 4(HeavyBlunt) = 10.3. DR: Medium vs HeavyBlunt = 1. Net damage: 9.3.
Ehrenwall Health 5 → Wound 1 (9.3 > 5). Health resets to 5.

**But** Establish Distance: Ehrenwall's successes (4.6) > Haelmund's (2.3) → Range changes to Far zone. Ehrenwall gains initiative.

Ehrenwall gets range advantage but takes a Wound. Trade completed.

### State Delta: Round 1
Ehrenwall: Wound 1/3, Health reset to 5. Pool: 14−1 = **13D** (new wound system: −1D). Stamina 8→7.
Haelmund: Stamina 5→4. At Far zone, HeavyBlunt Short weapon → **Haelmund cannot attack at Far zone.** He must Establish Distance back to Close.

## Round 2 — Ehrenwall attacks, Haelmund forced to Establish Distance

Ehrenwall (initiative): sees Haelmund must Establish Distance (forced action at wrong range). Ehrenwall commits to Strike.

Ehrenwall Strike: 13D (post-wound) split 8D Off / 5D Def (TN7).
Haelmund Establish Distance: all dice to the attempt. 11D TN7 vs Ehrenwall's Offence.

Ehrenwall Off expected net: 8×0.33 = 2.6 (at TN6 HeavyCut): 8×(0.5−0.1) = 3.2. 
Haelmund Distance attempt: 11D TN7. Expected net: 3.6.

**Haelmund's Establish Distance:** succeeds if 3.6 ≥ Ehrenwall's Offence successes (3.2) AND not hit.
Haelmund not hit ← Ehrenwall's 8D (TN6) vs Haelmund's Defence (all allocated to Establish Distance → 0D defence).

Ehrenwall hit chance: P(3.2 vs 0 = essentially P(≥1 net)) ≈ 94%. Haelmund hit.

Ehrenwall damage on Haelmund: Excess ~3.2 + STR4 + 4(HeavyCut) = 11.2. DR(Heavy vs HeavyCut) = 5. Net damage: 6.2.
Haelmund Health 4 → Wound 1 (6.2 > 4). Health resets to 4. Pool: 11−1 = **10D** (−1D wound).

Haelmund Establish Distance: requires successes ≥ Ehrenwall's Offence (3.2) AND not hit. Since Haelmund is hit: Establish Distance **fails** (hit while attempting).

Range stays Far zone. Haelmund forced to try again next round.

### State Delta: Round 2
Ehrenwall: Pool 13D, Wound 1/3, Stamina 7→6.
Haelmund: Pool 10D, Wound 1/3, Stamina 4→3. Still at Far zone, cannot attack.
Ehrenwall retains initiative (hit + not hit = initiative transfer).

Ehrenwall retains initiative.

### State Delta: Round 3

Haelmund: Pool 9D, Wounds 2/3, Stamina 2/5. Still Far zone.

## Round 4 — Haelmund near-incapacitation

Haelmund: Wounds 2/3 (Max wounds 3 → needs one more). Pool 9D.
Haelmund Stamina = 2. He must Take a Breath or risk Out of Breath next round (Stamina depletes 1/round any action taken: 2→1 next round, then forced Out of Breath).

**Haelmund decision:** Full Guard (all dice Defence) — buys time, doesn't try Distance. But Full Guard gives Ehrenwall +2D Offence.

Ehrenwall strikes with +2D: 13+2 = 15D Off. Haelmund Full Guard: 9D Def (TN8 — HeavyBlunt def TN).

Wait — Full Guard: "All dice to Defence. Opponent gains +2D Offence." Haelmund's 9D full defence at TN8. Expected net: 9×(0.3−0.1) = 1.8 (TN8: P(die≥8)=0.3, −P(1)=0.1).
Ehrenwall 15D Off at TN6: 15×0.4 = 6.0 expected net.

Net hits to Haelmund: 6.0−1.8 = 4.2. Critical hit threshold: net ≥3 → crit (weapon modifier doubled). 4.2 ≥ 3 → **Critical hit**.
Damage: 4.2 + STR4 + (4×2 crit) = 4.2+4+8 = 16.2. DR(Heavy vs HeavyCut)=5. Net: 11.2.
Haelmund Health 4 → Wound 3 → **Incapacitated** (reaches max wounds).

### State Delta: Round 4
Ehrenwall: Pool 13D, Wound 1, Stamina 5→4. **Combat over.**
Haelmund: **Incapacitated.** Wounds 3/3.

## Wound System Validation (new −1D)

| Round | Ehrenwall Pool | Haelmund Pool | Note |
|-------|--------------|--------------|-------|
| 0 | 14D | 11D | Starting |
| 1 | 13D (W1) | 11D | Ehrenwall wounded establishing range |
| 2 | 13D | 10D (W1) | Haelmund hit |
| 3 | 13D | 9D (W2) | Second wound |
| 4 | 13D | 9D→incapacitated | Crit on Full Guard |

**Ehrenwall's 1 wound (−1D: 14→13D) had minimal tactical impact** — she retained full offensive capability. The wound reduced her pool by 7%, which is mechanically present but not fight-defining. Consistent with design intent.

**Haelmund's wound cascade (11→10→9D over 2 rounds)** was more significant — he was already losing; the pool reduction made the Establish Distance attempts marginally harder each round (~3.6→3.3 expected). Not decisive (he was going to lose anyway) but correctly reflects accumulating degradation.

**Finding F-23 [P3]:** Under −1D system, wounds create a smooth degradation curve. No single wound creates a fight-ending cliff effect. Correct — dramatic thresholds remain visible through pool reduction rather than sudden Ob jumps.

**Finding F-24 [P2]:** Range mismatch (Long vs Short at wrong range) is the decisive factor, not pool size. Haelmund could never attack. Range control is more fight-determining than any wound modifier. Ehrenwall's tactical sophistication (establishing Far zone immediately) dominated.

## Inquisitor Vald CE Trigger

Inquisitor Vald (CE=2) witnesses:
- Full combat with a practitioner-scholar (Maret Uln) present as the subject of detention
- If Maret performs any passive Thread perception during the confrontation: Vald gains +1 CE (interrogating practitioner with passive perception present)
- The violence itself: no CE from combat (CE triggers are Thread-related)
- Vald does NOT gain CE from watching a sword fight

If Maret performs a passive Diagnosis of the scene (checking Vald's thread configuration out of self-preservation instinct): Vald CE 2→3. At CE 3: TS growth check triggered. Spirit TN7 Ob2 (essentialist formation). Vald's Spirit (estimated 3 for Inquisitor profile): 3D, P(≥1 net at TN7) ≈ 63%, but Ob2 applies: P(≥2 net) ≈ 26%. Most likely: no TS growth this event. But Vald is now at threshold for future triggering.

**Finding F-25 [P3]:** CE accumulation is slow for passive exposure (Vald would need 2 more events at +1 CE each to reach the Conversion trajectory). The Inquisitor arc is a multi-session trajectory, not a single-scene event. Mechanically correct — institutional formation creates inertia.

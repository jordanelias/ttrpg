## RS Threshold Risk Assessment

Current RS: 20. After W-06b Success: RS 22−2 = 20.
Next session with any Thread operations: likely −3 to −5 more (standard Weaving operations). RS could reach 17 (below 20) — entering the lower Stirring band.

**What happens at RS < 20 (approaching 0–19, Dormant band)?** Actually RS bands count DOWN from 100. Re-reading stage5: "0–19: Dormant" is the lowest band. RS starts high and falls. We're at RS 22, band is Stirring (20–39). Falling further drops into Dormant (0–19).

**Wait — re-read RS thresholds (stage5):**
> 0–19: Dormant → "Thread practice is folklore. Leap fails automatically."
> 20–39: Stirring → "Practitioners surface. Shifting Objects reported."

**Finding F-58 [P1 — critical]:** RS is currently 22, in the Stirring band. If RS drops below 20 (into Dormant), **the Leap fails automatically for all practitioners.** At RS 20, a single failed Weaving (−2 RS) would push RS to 18 — into Dormant. At that point, no practitioner in the world can perform Thread operations until RS rises above 20 again.

This is an existential risk from ordinary Thread use at RS 22. The margin is razor-thin. Every Thread operation risks crossing into Dormant, which paralyses all practitioners globally.

**RS management required:** Players should be aware that RS at 22 means they are 2 RS points from operational shutdown. This is not a P1 design flaw — it's correct campaign tension — but the simulation confirms it is a real constraint at canonical starting RS 28 after a few sessions of operations.

# SIM-X-15: Knot Crisis — Klapp Under Coherence Strain + Call a Knot
## Mode: C + D (Scenario + edge: Knot crisis threshold, Call a Knot, Certainty loss cascade)
## Mechanics: §4.5 Coherence × §4.7 Knots × §4.6 Certainty × §9 Social × §13 NPCs
## Context: Klapp (Coherence 9, Dissonant band) is being interrogated by Cardinal Olafsson (reinstated after a partial political recovery) about his archive activities. Klapp's primary Close Knot is Brother Ansel — a young monk he has mentored for 12 years who is also assigned to the archive. Ansel has been sensing wrongness for 3 sessions (Dissonant Knot strain: +1 per 3 sessions). This session Ansel's wrongness strain reaches his Close Knot wrongness threshold.

---

## Knot State Construction

**Klapp's Close Knot: Brother Ansel**
- Knot type: Close. Wrongness threshold: 3 strain. Crisis threshold: 6 strain.
- Current strain: 3 (reached wrongness threshold this session — 1 strain per 3 sessions at Dissonant, 3 sessions elapsed).
- At wrongness threshold: "Ansel begins perceiving something is off about Klapp. Manifests as uncanniness — something about Klapp is wrong but Ansel cannot name it."

**Klapp's Coherence:** 9 (Dissonant band: 10–8). Effects: "Close Knots sense wrongness (+1 strain per 3 sessions)" — confirming above.

**Klapp's Certainty:** Starting = Spirit score = 3. No losses yet. Certainty: 3/3.

**Olafsson (Interrogator):**
- Reinstated after partial political recovery (Church Stability now 4; Olafsson partially active).
- Composure: 7. Cognition: 4, Presence: 5. Resonant Style: Evidence.
- Interrogation: this is not a formal Debate — it is an Inquisitorial procedure (asymmetric: Olafsson interrogates, Klapp answers). Olafsson has procedural authority.
- Pool: Cognition + History Ecclesiastical Law (2) = 4+5 = 9D TN7.

## Phase 1: Interrogation (Social Scene)

**Olafsson's approach:** Evidence style (his resonant style). He has recovered some of the archive access records that the Inquisitor file-building identified.

**Klapp's position:** He must answer without revealing his TS development or his Conversion trajectory. His dominant conviction is now fracturing — between institutional loyalty (Faith, trained since youth) and what he now knows to be true (Thread practice is not heresy).

**Klapp pool:** Cognition (4) + History Theology (3) = 4+6 = 10D TN7. Coherence 9 (Dissonant): no mechanical penalty yet (penalties start at Fragmented, 4–3).

**Exchange 1:** Olafsson Evidence (archive records) vs Klapp Consequence (ecclesiastical precedent for scholarly access).

Style mismatch: Klapp uses Consequence vs Olafsson's Evidence audience (Olafsson's resonant style is Evidence — Klapp using Consequence gets no resonant bonus). Klapp using Consequence to an Evidence-resonant NPC: no penalty (styles are free to use; only resonant bonus is lost).

Olafsson: 9D+1D (Evidence to Evidence-resonant: no bonus for him either since the +1D is about addressing the NPC using their resonant style — Olafsson is addressing Klapp, not using his own resonant style to address himself). Clarification: resonant bonus applies when *addressing* the NPC using *their* style. Olafsson addressing Klapp: Klapp's resonant style is unknown here (we haven't established Klapp's resonant style). Assume Consequence (scholars are forward-thinkers). Olafsson using Evidence (not Consequence) → no resonant bonus. Olafsson: 9D.

Klapp: 10D. Klapp has slight edge. P(Klapp wins E1) ≈ 55%. Most likely: Klapp wins. Olafsson strain +1.

## Phase 2: Ansel Arrives (Knot Crisis trigger)

**Mid-scene:** Brother Ansel enters to deliver a document. He sees Klapp under interrogation. Ansel's wrongness strain reaches threshold (3) this session — "Ansel begins perceiving something is off about Klapp."

**Mechanical effect:** At wrongness threshold, the Knot entity "takes action — confrontation, departure, or betrayal" is the Crisis threshold (6 strain), not the wrongness threshold. At wrongness (3 strain): Ansel begins perceiving wrongness, but the Knot is not in Crisis. No forced action yet.

**However:** Ansel's presence creates a social dynamic. Olafsson notices Ansel's reaction to Klapp — the unease is visible. Olafsson can now use this as evidence.

**Exchange 2:** Olafsson shifts to Character style — "Even your closest companion senses something wrong with you, Cardinal." Addressing Klapp with Character style: Klapp's resonant style (assumed Consequence) → Olafsson gets no resonant bonus. But: this is an Inspiration attack. Olafsson names Klapp's Knot (Ansel) as the target.

**Inspiration attack procedure:** "Attacker declares Character Style and names a specific Inspiration as target — if the defending orator achieves net ≤ 0 (actual Failure), the named Inspiration loses 1 point."

Klapp must have Inspiration that can be targeted. Inspiration tracks are separate from Knots — but the attack uses the Knot as narrative leverage. If Klapp's Inspiration includes "Ansel's trust in me" (GM determines Inspiration content; assume Klapp has Inspiration value 2 tied to his mentorship role): Inspiration 2 is at risk.

Olafsson Character: 9D TN7. Klapp defence: 10D TN7.
Expected nets: 3.0 vs 3.3. Very close. P(Olafsson wins) ≈ 47%.

**Most likely: Klapp wins Exchange 2 (slight edge), Olafsson strain 2.**

Klapp's Inspiration is safe (Klapp did not achieve net ≤ 0).

## Phase 3: Klapp's Dilemma — Call a Knot?

**Exchange 3 (final in Formal Debate):** Olafsson has strain 2 (still far from Composure 7). Klapp needs to end this without Olafsson filing additional accusations. Klapp considers calling the Ansel Knot to boost his roll.

**Call a Knot mechanics:**
- +3D to the roll. Cost: +2 strain to the Knot.
- Ansel's Knot current strain: 3 (at wrongness threshold). Using it: strain 3+2 = 5. Below Crisis threshold (6). Knot does not enter Crisis. But: strain 5 of 6 crisis threshold means one more use would trigger Crisis.

**Klapp calls Ansel Knot:** +3D to Exchange 3 pool. Pool: 10+3 = 13D TN7. Expected net: 13×0.33 = 4.3. Olafsson: 9D, expected 3.0. P(Klapp wins E3) ≈ 66%.

**Coherence cost of Knot call:** None. Calling a Knot is a social mechanic, not a Thread operation. No Coherence impact.

**Certainty cost:** Klapp is invoking a relationship built on deception (Ansel trusts Klapp, but Klapp is concealing his Conversion). "Non-consensual Thread work on sentient being: −1 Certainty." Calling a Knot is not Thread work. However: the GM may rule Certainty −1 for using a relationship instrumentally in a way that violates its trust. This is a narrative Certainty cost, not a mechanical one in the rules. No Certainty loss from the Call itself per written rules.

Most likely: Klapp wins Exchange 3. Olafsson strain 3. Debate ends 3–0 Klapp.

### Debate outcome
Klapp wins 3–0. Olafsson does not file additional accusations this season. Inquisitor investigation stalls.

**But:** Ansel Knot strain is now 5/6 Crisis threshold. One more Call or one more Knot-strain-producing event tips it to Crisis.

## Certainty Loss Check (Leap-based)

Klapp has performed Thread operations in prior sessions (X-09, X-11). Each successful Leap costs −1 Certainty. Klapp has Leaped at least twice (X-09 W-41, X-11 passive perception). Certainty: 3 − 2 = **1**.

At Certainty 1: "One loss from Rendering Crisis." At Rendering Crisis: Certainty 0 — "The character's rendering of reality has become unstable. Must resolve the dissonance narratively."

**Finding F-62 [P2]:** Klapp's Certainty is 1 after two successful Leaps (starting Certainty = Spirit = 3). He is one Leap away from Rendering Crisis. Any Thread operation he performs will trigger this. Given his Conversion trajectory, he is approaching a forced narrative event.

**Interaction: Coherence 9 + Certainty 1 simultaneously.** Both tracks at near-crisis. This is the correct arc for a practitioner deep in the institutional Church — the cognitive dissonance of what he knows vs. what he has devoted his life to is mechanically present.

## Knot Crisis Projection

**If Ansel reaches Crisis threshold (strain 6):**
- Ansel "takes action — confrontation, departure, or betrayal."
- Klapp's Close Knot in Crisis cannot be Called.
- Klapp loses his primary social resource.
- Ansel may report Klapp's behaviour to Olafsson (betrayal trajectory) — completing the Inquisitor file that was stalled.

**Probability of Crisis this season:** Knot strain sources active this season:
- Coherence 9 Dissonant: +1 strain per 3 sessions. If 3 sessions this season: +1 strain. Ansel strain: 5→6 → **Crisis**.

**Finding F-63 [P2]:** At Coherence 9 (Dissonant), Knot strain accumulation is slow (1 per 3 sessions) but Ansel is already at strain 5/6. The Crisis is near-inevitable by end of season without active strain recovery. Strain recovery requires "active, positive engagement with the Knot" (−1 strain per season for Close Knots). Klapp would need to dedicate a Domain/personal action to strengthening the Ansel relationship — but his Conversion makes authentic engagement increasingly difficult.

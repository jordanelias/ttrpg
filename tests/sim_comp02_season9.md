## SCENE 1 — "Three Voices, One Chamber"
### Multi-Party Contest (FIRST TEST of this mechanic)
### Actors: Ravn (Hafenmark), Confessor Mads (Church), Church Institutional Speaker (NPC)
### Topic: RS instability — is the Church responsible?

**Setup:** A Parliamentary session. Three parties contest simultaneously: Ravn (arguing Church AER manipulation), Mads (arguing the Church is lying about RS — sincere, devastating, suicidal), and a Church Institutional Speaker (Cog 6, Att 5, History 8 → argue pool 20D) defending doctrine.

**Multi-Party Contest rule:** GAP resolved — design_v1.md F-5 identified this as structural gap. Applying PP-280 (new, this session):

**PP-280 — Multi-Party Contest Procedure:**
- Each party Appraises once at exchange open. Appraise results are shared (all parties see all reads).
- Choose: each party picks genre/orientation independently.
- Argue: lowest Attunement declares first; others hear, then declare in Att ascending order; highest Att declares last.
- Resolve: for each pair of same-genre parties, apply CLASH or AMPLIFY. For different-genre pairs, apply CROSS. All movements calculated simultaneously. Apply net to Conviction Track.
- Conviction Track: three-way track. Two tracks in play: Track A-B (Church vs Ravn), Track A-C (Church vs Mads). A win on either track advances that orator's position.

**Step 1 — Appraise (shared results):**
- Ravn: Att(3) → 3D TN7 Ob1. P(≥1)≈74%. **Pass.** Reads Church speaker: Past/Obscuring.
- Mads: Att(4) → 4D TN7 Ob1. P(≥1)≈87%. **Pass.** Reads Church speaker: Past/Obscuring. Also reads Ravn: Present/Revealing (correctly).
- Church NPC: Att(5) → 5D TN7 Ob1. P(≥1)≈95%. **Overwhelming.** Reads both Ravn (Present/Revealing) AND one specific detail: Ravn is citing Reformed Settlement text (Memory bonus incoming).

**Step 2 — Choose:**
- Ravn: **Past/Revealing** (non-optimal — audience is mixed Parliament, Crown seats favour Present; but Ravn argues from principle, not pragmatism. He will use Past because the Reformed Settlement is a past document.)
- Mads: **Present/Revealing** (sincere — "what is happening now to the RS is the Church's fault"). Optimal for Crown-weighted audience.
- Church NPC: **Past/Obscuring** (doctrine, institutional authority).

**Step 3 — Argue (Att order: Ravn 3, Mads 4, Church NPC 5):**

Ravn declares first: Past/Revealing. Memory bonus (citing Reformed Settlement): +2D.
Pool: Cog(5)×2 + Parliamentary History(8) + 2D Memory = **20D** | TN 7.
E[net]: 20×0.30 = **6.0**

Mads declares second (hears Ravn is Past/Revealing): Present/Revealing.
Pool: Cog(3)×2 + Parish Confessor History(7) = **13D** | TN 7.
E[net]: 13×0.30 = **3.9**

Church NPC declares last (hears both): Past/Obscuring (holds ground).
Pool: 20D. E[net]: **6.0**

**Step 4 — Resolve (simultaneous):**

Audience: mixed Parliament. Dominant: Crown seats (Virtue Ethics → Present boosted) + Hafenmark (Categorical Imperative → Past boosted). Split audience: Past weight = 1.0 (Hafenmark), Present weight = 1.5 (Crown). Use average: Past=1.0, Present=1.25 for this mixed session. Resistance = ceil(avg Stability(3,5)/4) = ceil(4/4) = **1** (PP-278: resistance = ceil(Stability/4)).

**Track A-B (Church NPC vs Ravn):** Both Past. Ravn Revealing, Church Obscuring → **CLASH.**
Margin: Ravn E[net] 6.0 vs Church E[net] 6.0 → expected margin 0. Near coin-flip.
If Ravn wins by M: movement = ⌊(M × 1.0 × 1.0) − 1⌋. M=1 → ⌊0⌋=0; M=2 → 1; M=3 → 2.
If Church wins by M: movement = ⌊(M × 1.0 × 0.5) − 1⌋. M=1 → ⌊−0.5⌋=0; M=2 → 0; M=3 → ⌊0.5⌋=0. Church needs M≥4 to move track. **Church's Obscuring orientation fails against resistance 1.**

**Most likely: near-zero movement on Track A-B. Ravn and Church cancel.**

**Track A-C (Church NPC vs Mads):** Different genres (Church Past, Mads Present) → **CROSS** (PP-245 simultaneous).
Mads movement: E[net] 3.9, genre Present weight 1.25, orientation Revealing 1.0. ⌊(3.9×1.25) − 1⌋ = ⌊3.875⌋ = **3** toward Mads.
Church counter: E[net] 6.0, genre Past weight 1.0, orientation Obscuring 0.5. ⌊(6.0×0.5) − 1⌋ = ⌊2⌋ = **2** toward Church.
Net Track A-C: +3 − 2 = **+1 toward Mads.** Track A-C: 5→6. (Mads leads narrowly; threshold for win ≥7.)

**Dual win-condition check (ED-012):**
- Exchange majority: Mads won Track A-C exchange (+1). Ravn and Church tied Track A-B.
- Audience capture: neither orator has threshold yet.

**Non-optimal consequence:** Ravn chose Past when Present would have given weight 1.25 (vs 1.0). His CLASH with Church was near-even rather than Ravn-favoured. The principle cost him 0.25 genre weight — small but real.

**Mads' sincerity:** Mads used Present/Revealing — the optimal choice for this audience — but not because he calculated it. He chose it because it's true. His non-optimal trait (tells the truth) accidentally produced the mechanically correct choice.

**State delta:**
- Track A-B: 5 (stalemate, no movement)
- Track A-C: 6 (Mads leads; threshold ≥7 needed for Mads win)
- Composure: all intact (exchange 1, no Partial results)
- Ravn CP: +1 (Belief 1 pursued)
- Mads CP: +1 (Belief 1 pursued — will lose everything for this)

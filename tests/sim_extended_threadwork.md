## TEST 7: Knot-Mediated Remote Investigation (Surveillance)

### Setup

Edda (TS 72) has a Close Knot with Brother Aldric (the Cathedral archivist from prior sims).
Disposition +5 (Bonded). Knot formed during a previous season.

Edda is in Sigurdshelm (T12). Aldric is in Himmelenger (T9). Edda wants to Thread-Read
Aldric's current Thread configuration remotely — specifically to perceive what documents
Aldric is currently examining (the Thread configuration of Aldric's consciousness retains
traces of recently-perceived content).

### Procedure

**Knot-mediated Thread-Read.** Edda Leaps and directs her intentionality through the Knot
connection to Aldric. She is perceiving Aldric's configuration, not the Cathedral's.

**Scale:** Personal (one person's Thread configuration). Ob: 2. TN: 7.
**Pool:** 27D (Edda's Thread-Read pool). No distance modifier on Ob — the Knot IS the
connection. Distance does not add resistance; the Thread structure connecting them is
constitutive, not spatial.
**Knot cost:** +1 Knot strain per use (the relationship bears force from being used
as an intelligence channel). Aldric may feel a vague sense of being observed — at TS 0,
he perceives nothing conscious, but the relational thread vibrates.

P(≥2 at 27D TN7): ~99%. Trivially easy.

### Consequences

**Evidence yield:** Edda perceives what Aldric is reading. If it is investigation-relevant:
Evidence Track +2. Reliability tag: Thread-verified. Inert Knowledge for non-sensitives.

**Knot strain:** +1. If Edda does this repeatedly (once per season), the Knot accumulates
strain. At strain ≥ 3 (threshold depends on Knot strength): Knot Crisis. Aldric begins
feeling wrongness — the relationship feels contaminated. He may withdraw.

**Moral dimension:** This is surveillance. Aldric did not consent to being remotely perceived.
The Foundations do not assign moral valence to Thread operations (P-04: monstrosity is
ontological, not moral), but the social dimension is real. If Aldric learns (through
another practitioner's Diagnosis, or through his own intuition at the wrongness): Disposition
drops precipitously. Bonded → potentially Hostile.

**[FINDING KN-01]: Knot-mediated investigation is mechanically trivial and socially devastating.**
The Thread-Read is near-guaranteed at Personal scale (Ob 2 against a 27D pool). But each
use damages the relationship. The investigator must choose: intelligence at the cost of the
relationship, or the relationship at the cost of intelligence. This is P-12 (relational
contagion) operating in reverse — the practitioner's intentionality propagates through the
Knot, straining it.

**[FINDING KN-02]: Knot strain from investigative use is not defined in the threadwork system.**
The existing strain sources (Coherence degradation wrongness, Anchoring Scene cost, external
events) do not include "practitioner deliberately directs intentionality through Knot for
intelligence gathering." This is a new strain source.

**Proposed rule (fieldwork §2.4):** "Knot-mediated Thread-Read: +1 Knot strain per use.
The Knotted party may detect the intrusion: Spirit check TN 7, Ob = practitioner's
Cognition ÷ 2 (the practitioner's skill at concealing their intentionality). If the Knotted
party detects: Disposition −3 (betrayal of relational trust). The Knot itself does not
break from investigative use — Knots are constitutive, not contractual. But the relationship
that the Knot expresses may be destroyed."

## TEST 8: Community Weaving × Fieldwork

### Setup

The Restoration Movement performs Community Weaving in Oastad (T13, Varfell-controlled).
Restoration Presence marker is active. RS: 52. PT(T13): 2.

Community Weaving (PP-250 canonical): Pool = Attunement + History bonus + TPS. TN 7. Ob 3.
This is a Thread operation with co-movement.

### Investigation interaction

A PC investigating the Restoration's activities in T13 is conducting an Evidence Track
investigation: "What Thread operations has the Restoration performed in Oastad?"
(Complex, Threshold 5). Evidence Track: 2/5.

**The Community Weave itself is evidence.** When it fires:
- Temporal auto-effect: History Resonance (minor — the substrate's history shifts slightly).
- Epistemic auto-effect: observers at TS 30+ perceive the operation (per observation table).
- Actualized auto-effect: d6 table (environmental consequence — sensory anomaly, object shift, etc.).

**A TS 30+ investigator present during Community Weaving** perceives it directly. Thread-Read
is unnecessary — the operation IS the evidence. Evidence Track: +2 (observed Thread operation
= Verified evidence, because the investigator perceived it first-hand).

**A non-sensitive investigator present** perceives the actualized auto-effect only (d6 table).
If d6 = 3 ("nearby person feels disorientation"): the investigator experiences Dissonance.
Spirit check TN 7 vs Dissonance Factor 1 (Thread operation nearby, brief).

If Spirit check succeeds (likely at Spirit 3+): the investigator senses something happened.
Evidence: +1 (Partial — direction but not detail). Tag: Observational.

If Spirit check fails: Spirit −1D for rest of scene. The investigator knows something
happened but cannot articulate it. Evidence: +0 (too disoriented to process).

**[FINDING CW-01]: Community Weaving is inherently detectable by fieldwork.** The co-movement
auto-effects make every Community Weave a potential evidence source for investigators.
Non-sensitives gain partial evidence from actualized effects. Sensitives gain full evidence
from direct perception. The Restoration cannot Weave in secret from a TS 30+ investigator
in the same territory.

**Church Attention Pool interaction:** If the investigator reports the Community Weaving to
the Church: +1 AP in T13. If the investigator is Church-affiliated: automatic report (NPC AI).
If the investigator is Varfell: strategic choice — report (gains Church favour, damages
Restoration) or conceal (protects Restoration alliance, risks Church discovering Varfell
knew and concealed).

**[FINDING CW-02]: Community Weaving + fieldwork creates faction intelligence trade-offs.**
The information gained from detecting Community Weaving is valuable to MULTIPLE factions
with competing interests. The investigator's faction loyalty determines what they do with
the evidence. This is the fieldwork system producing political gameplay from Thread mechanics.

## TEST 9: Threadcut Being × Fieldwork

### Setup

A threadcut being (Mode 3 — see Foundations §5.3) has been encountered in the Southernmost
(T15). The being maintains its rendering through continuous Thread work. It does not Leap —
it is already in the originary state. It has no Coherence track. Its operations cost +1
Rendering Strain each (accelerating De-Actualisation).

Edda (TS 72) attempts to investigate the being: "What is this entity's origin and purpose?"
(Structural, Threshold 8).

### Thread-Read on a Threadcut Being

**The being's Thread configuration is visible to Edda (TS 72 → "Full perception. The observer
perceives the being's self-rendering effort — the continuous work that holds it in place.").**

Thread-Read pool: 27D. TN 7. Ob: 5 (Depth 4 — Liminal, active Thread phenomena).
The being IS active Thread phenomena. Its existence is a continuous Thread operation.

P(≥5 at 27D TN7): ~92%. Near-certain.

**Evidence yield:** +2 (Success). Edda perceives the being's Thread structure. It has no
temporal depth — no accumulated past. Its configuration is radically present. P-06 fires:
"Past-Pull against threadcut being auto-produces Gap. No temporal thread to pull."

**[FINDING TC-01]: A threadcut being's Thread configuration is investigative evidence of a
unique ontological kind.** The configuration reveals: (a) the being has no organic spooling
(no layer-1 Ein Sof connection per P-15), (b) it maintains itself through continuous layer-3
threadwork, (c) its "memories" are maintained performances, not temporal accumulations.
This is evidence about the NATURE OF BEING — the investigation produces philosophical knowledge,
not just factual knowledge. This is the ontical/ontological distinction (Foundations §17)
made mechanical: the investigator accesses ontological knowledge through Thread perception.

### The Being Operates on the Investigator

The threadcut being perceives Edda's Thread-Read. It has full Thread awareness (it is always
in the originary state). It may respond with its own Thread operation — directed at Edda.

**External Thread operation by the being:** No Leap required. Standard pool: Spirit + History
+ TPS. Cost: +1 Rendering Strain.

If the being Weaves a Thread concealment on itself (hiding its configuration from further
perception): Edda's subsequent Thread-Reads face +Ob = being's self-concealment degree.

If the being Pulls Edda's recent memory of the Thread-Read (attempting to make her forget
what she perceived): this is a memory Pull (P-09 applies — produces orphaned configuration,
detectable, co-movement fires). Ob: 2 (standard Pull on recent memory). The being's pool
is likely 15-20D at TN 7. P(≥2): ~90%.

**If the being succeeds:** Edda's memory of the Thread-Read is displaced. She has an orphaned
configuration — she cannot remember what she perceived. But the orphaned configuration is
detectable by any TS 30+ practitioner. Edda herself, examining her own Thread configuration,
can perceive the Gap in her own memory. She knows something was taken, even if she cannot
remember what.

**Evidence consequence:** Evidence Track DOES NOT REVERSE. The evidence was gathered and
recorded (the Thread-Read resolved). The Being's Pull displaced Edda's conscious memory of
the evidence, but the Evidence Track is a player-level mechanic (the player knows what was
found). The character cannot access the memory without help (Mending to restore the orphaned
configuration, or a second Thread-Read to re-perceive).

**[FINDING TC-02]: A threadcut being can counter-investigate the investigator.** The being's
Thread operations can displace, conceal, or alter evidence that the investigator has gathered.
This is contested investigation (fieldwork §4.6) at its most extreme — the target of
investigation is itself a Thread-capable entity that can fight back below the level of
consciousness. The investigation becomes a Thread-level chess match.

**[FINDING TC-03]: The Evidence Track's player-level persistence is philosophically justified.**
The Evidence Track does not represent the character's memory — it represents the accumulated
state of knowledge about the investigation question. A character whose memory is Pulled still
HAS the evidence (written notes, shared knowledge with allies, physical artifacts). The
Track's persistence is a rendering-level fact: the knowledge exists even if one character's
access to it is disrupted. This is the distinction between knowledge (which persists in the
rendered world) and consciousness of knowledge (which can be Thread-manipulated).

### The Being as Source of Information

The threadcut being may also CHOOSE to share information. If Edda achieves Disposition +3
(Trusting) with the being through social fieldwork (Read, Converse, Connect — yes, social
actions work on threadcut beings, because social interaction is rendering-level, and the
being maintains a rendering), the being may volunteer information about its origin.

This information is UNIQUE — it is testimony from a being that existed before the Calamity,
or that arrived through the tear. No other source can provide this perspective. Evidence tag:
Testimonial (the being is a witness). The testimony is not Thread-verified (it is rendered
communication, not Thread perception). Non-sensitives can receive and act on it. This is the
most powerful evidence in the game: a first-person account from the other side of the rendering
boundary, delivered in ontical form (communication) rather than ontological form (Thread perception).

**[FINDING TC-04]: Social fieldwork with threadcut beings produces uniquely valuable evidence.**
The being's testimony is the only source of information about what lies beyond the rendering
boundary that is transmissible to non-sensitives (because the being chooses to render its
knowledge into communicable form). The epistemological barrier (P-08) does not block this
pathway — the being is performing the translation itself. P-08 blocks non-sensitive PERCEPTION
of Thread-level reality, not communication FROM a Thread-capable entity. The being is a
bridge across the barrier — and building the social relationship (Disposition track) to access
this bridge is an investigation arc in itself.

## TEST 10: Mending Arc as Multi-Session Investigation Prerequisite

### Setup

The Locked Zone beneath Sigurdshelm blocks access to the archive's deepest chamber.
Investigation of its contents requires Mending the Locked Zone border to open access.

**Mending pool (Edda):** 18D (Att 6 + Foc 5 + TPS 7). TN 7.
**Locked Zone border Ob:** 8+ (per params_threadwork.md §Mending). With 2 Momentum: effective
Ob 6. P(≥6 at 18D TN7): ~45%.

### Multi-Session Mending Arc

Each Mending attempt:
- Consumes 1 fieldwork scene action.
- Costs Coherence −1 (base Mending cost).
- On Success: +1 RS. Gap reduced one severity.
- On Failure: −2 RS. Coherence −1.

**Locked Zone border → Catastrophic Gap → Entrenched Gap → Standard Gap → Micro-Gap → Closed.**

Each reduction takes one successful Mending. Total: 4 successful Mendings to fully open
the passage (Locked border through four severity reductions).

At 45% success per attempt, expected attempts to get 4 successes: ~9 attempts.
Coherence cost: −1 per attempt (success or fail). Over 9 attempts: Coherence −9.
Edda starts at Coherence 5. After 5 attempts: Coherence 0 (Rendering Crisis).

**[FINDING MA-01]: Solo Mending of a Locked Zone drives the practitioner to Rendering Crisis.**
The Coherence budget (5 remaining for Edda) supports only 5 Mending attempts. At 45% success,
expected successes in 5 attempts: ~2.25. Not enough to fully open the passage.

**Solutions:**
1. **Collective Mending:** Multiple practitioners share the Coherence cost. Each participant
   pays −1 per operation. With 3 practitioners: 15 total Coherence budget. Expected 7
   successes in 15 attempts. Enough.
2. **Multi-season approach:** Mend 2-3 times per season. Recover Coherence between seasons
   (full season non-practice: +1; Anchoring Scenes: +1 each). A practitioner at Coherence 5
   can Mend 3 times (→ Coherence 2), then recover 2-3 Coherence over one season, then Mend
   again. Total timeline: 3-4 seasons.
3. **Warden assistance:** At Warden Cooperation 3, Edeyja actively Mends. RS +2/season. The
   Wardens may assist with the Locked Zone Mending directly, adding their pool to the
   collective.

**Investigation integration:** Each successful Mending opens the passage further. After each
reduction, a Thread-Read through the thinned barrier reveals more of the archive's contents.
Evidence Track advances incrementally:

| Severity Reduction | Evidence Access | Progress |
|-------------------|----------------|----------|
| Locked → Catastrophic | Faint Thread impressions through barrier. +1 Evidence (direction). |
| Catastrophic → Entrenched | Thread configurations partially visible. +2 Evidence (detail). |
| Entrenched → Standard | Substantial perception possible. +2 Evidence (actionable). |
| Standard → Closed | Full access. Thread-Read at will. +3 Evidence (complete). |

Total Evidence from Mending arc: +8. Enough to resolve a Structural investigation (Threshold 8).

**[FINDING MA-02]: The Mending arc IS the investigation.** The evidence is not gathered AFTER
the passage is opened — it is gathered DURING the Mending process. Each severity reduction
reveals more. The investigation and the Mending are the same activity performed simultaneously.
This is the fieldwork system's deepest integration with threadwork: the practitioner's repair
work produces knowledge as a constitutive consequence, because repairing the substrate requires
perceiving its structure, and perceiving its structure IS gathering evidence.

**[FINDING MA-03]: Mending arcs create natural campaign pacing.** A 3-4 season Mending arc with
investigation milestones at each reduction creates a built-in campaign structure. Each season
produces new evidence (advancing the Evidence Track) and new consequences (Coherence loss,
RS changes, faction responses to ongoing Thread operations in a sensitive location). The GM
has 3-4 seasons of content driven by a single investigation question.

## TEST 11: Dissonance Checks on Non-Sensitive Fieldwork Partners

### Setup

Torsten (TS 0, Spirit 3) accompanies Edda during Thread operations. Multiple Thread ops
fire in his vicinity over the course of a season's fieldwork.

### Dissonance accumulation

Per PP-607/PP-610: Spirit check TN 7 vs Dissonance Factor when Thread operations fire nearby.

| Event | Dissonance Factor | Torsten Spirit check (3D TN7) |
|-------|-------------------|-------------------------------|
| Thread-Read nearby (brief) | 1 | P(≥1) = 63%. Likely passes. |
| Thread-Read nearby (significant — Structural scale) | 2 | P(≥2) = 31%. Likely fails. |
| POP affecting Torsten's own memories | 3 | P(≥3) = 10%. Almost certainly fails. |
| Entering Field-scale Gap | 2 | P(≥2) = 31%. |
| Extended time in Foundational zone | 4 | P(≥4) = 2%. Guaranteed failure. |

**Failure consequence:** Spirit −1D for rest of scene (acute) or season (sustained).
At Spirit 3: one failure → effective Spirit 2 for subsequent checks. Compound failures
spiral — each failure makes the next more likely.

**TS 30+ on Success:** "readable Thread information (Discovery Event or Belief revision)."
Torsten has TS 0. This clause does not apply.

**[FINDING DIS-01]: Non-sensitive fieldwork partners degrade over sustained Thread exposure.**
Torsten's Spirit degrades with each failed Dissonance check. Over a season of 6-8 Thread
operations: expected 2-4 failed Spirit checks. Effective Spirit: 3 → 1-2 for end-of-season
checks. At Spirit 1: Dissonance Factor 1 check = P(≥1 at 1D TN7) = 40%. Torsten can barely
function near Thread operations.

**This is NOT mechanically incapacitating** — Spirit −1D is per scene, not permanent. But
it creates a narrative arc: Torsten's capacity to accompany Edda erodes over the campaign.
He can still investigate at Depth 1-2 (no Thread exposure), but deep fieldwork with a
practitioner becomes increasingly costly.

**Recovery:** Spirit −1D clears at scene/season end. No permanent damage. But each season
of Thread-adjacent fieldwork produces the same pattern. Torsten is not permanently harmed,
but he is periodically impaired at precisely the moments when he is most needed (during
Thread operations that produce the deepest evidence).

**[FINDING DIS-02]: Dissonance creates natural field team rotation.** The optimal fieldwork
strategy is to rotate non-sensitive team members: Torsten operates for 3-4 Thread-adjacent
scenes, then withdraws while his Spirit recovers (scene change). A fresh non-sensitive
partner takes over. This creates a logistics dimension to deep investigation — the practitioner
needs multiple non-sensitive colleagues, not just one, for sustained operations.

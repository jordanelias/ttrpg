## SIM-FAC-01: Domain Action Calibration
Attacker pool = faction stat (1-7D). Ob = target stat (1-7).
Note: PC-involved actions add personal attribute to pool.

KEY CORRECTION: Unique action table had pool/Ob transposed for Crown Royal Decree.
Corrected: Crown Mandate 5D vs Ob 2 → P(Success+) = 60%, P(Overwhelm) = 23%.

Equal-stat NPC contest (4D vs Ob 4): 14% success, 59% partial, 27% fail.
DESIGN FINDING: NPC-only faction actions mostly produce Partial results.
PC involvement (personal attribute + faction stat) dramatically increases success.
This is intentional — creates meaningful PC agency in faction politics.

Ethical framework modifiers (±1/+2 Ob) produce ~15-20% success swing. Meaningful.

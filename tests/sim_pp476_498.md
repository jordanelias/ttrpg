## P1 FINDINGS

### P1-01 — PP-476: Crown Suppression + RM
**Setup:** 5P game. RM is the 5th player. Crown must suppress all other playable factions.
**Mechanism:** PP-476 condition: every other playable faction Mandate ≤ 2 OR eliminated OR Crown Treaty active. RM has no Mandate. No Crown Treaty mechanic defined for RM.
**Severity:** P1 — Crown victory unresolvable in 5P with RM.
**Frequency:** Every 5P game.
**Fix:** Add clause: "Restoration Movement always satisfies Crown suppression condition (RM has no institutional Mandate; they pose no parliamentary or military threat to Crown sovereignty)."

### P1-02 — PP-478: Assert Card Type Undefined
**Setup:** Church plays Assert (Soften) in Phase 4.
**Mechanism:** No card type specified. Church hand: 2× Senator, 1× Pontifex, 1× Consul, 1× Legionary, 1× Recess. Assert targets a territory (Consul-type) but operates on CV and Seizure (Senator-type). Ambiguous.
**Severity:** P1 — cannot execute without card assignment.
**Fix:** Assign Assert to Consul Inward (territorial administration action).

### P1-03 — PP-489: Morale Check Roll Procedure Undefined
**Setup:** Size 7 Command 3 unit deals damage.
**Mechanism:** PP-489 triggers "Morale check Ob +1." No base Ob, no dice pool defined for faction/mass combat Morale check.
**Severity:** P1 — mechanic cannot resolve.
**Fix:** Define: Morale check = Discipline pool vs Ob 1 (base). Size > Command adds +1 Ob (total Ob 2). Failure: Morale −1.

### P1-04 — PP-492: Cultural Uprising BG Pool Undefined
**Setup:** RM plays Pontifex card for Cultural Uprising in BG mode.
**Mechanism:** "Weaver Thread pool" is a TTRPG character stat composite (Attunement + History + TPS). No character exists in BG mode.
**Severity:** P1 — roll cannot be made in BG.
**Fix (PROVISIONAL):** BG Cultural Uprising pool = 1D per Presence marker in T9 (Himmelenger per victory_architecture_v1.md) + 1D per adjacent territory with Presence. Minimum 1D. Modifiers as stated.

### P1-05 — PP-493: Löwenritter Post-Coup Card Hand Undefined
**Setup:** Löwenritter plays Reconstitution (Senator Inward).
**Mechanism:** Post-coup Löwenritter card hand not defined in params_board_game.md. Senator Inward card type not in Löwenritter pre-coup hand.
**Severity:** P1 — Reconstitution unplayable without card.
**Fix:** Define Löwenritter post-coup hand. Provisional: 2× Legionary, 1× Senator, 1× Consul, 1× Praetor, 1× Recess. Adds Senator for Reconstitution.

### P1-06 — PP-497: Faction Morale Undefined
**Setup:** NPC kills or captures PC. PP-497 triggers "PC faction Morale Ob 2."
**Mechanism:** Factions have no Morale stat. Morale is a mass combat unit stat.
**Severity:** P1 — roll undefined.
**Fix:** Replace "PC faction Morale" with "PC faction Stability check, Ob 2." Fail: Military −1 one season.

### P1-07 — PP-498: Cardinal Death Suspension Scope
**Setup:** Cardinal of Justice dies.
**Mechanism:** PP-498 says "BG mechanics suspended 1 season." Does this mean all four Cardinals' mechanics or only Justice domain?
**Severity:** P1 — ambiguous scope makes implementation impossible.
**Fix:** Only the deceased Cardinal's domain mechanics are suspended. Fortitude: Templar deployment suspended. Justice: Inquisitor deployment suspended. Prudence: Tithes suspended. Temperance: AER maintenance suspended. Other Cardinals unaffected.

### P1-08 — D-05: Assert Overwhelming Bonus Unreachable
**Setup:** Church gets Assert Overwhelming on T14. Ob −1 modifier says "this season only." Same-territory Seizure prohibited same season.
**Mechanism:** Bonus cannot be used — it applies "this season only" but Seizure is banned same territory same season.
**Severity:** P1 — Overwhelming bonus is dead text.
**Fix:** Change "this season only" to "for the next Seizure of that territory in any season within 2 seasons of the Assert."

## P2 FINDINGS

### P2-01 — PP-477: Free Seizure Timing Not in Priority Order
**Mechanism:** Free seizure at PI ≤ 2 has no stated Priority in Phase 4. All Seizures appear to be Priority 6 (Unique Powers). Confirm.
**Provisional:** Free seizure fires at Priority 6, same as standard Seizure.

### P2-02 — PP-479: Negative Domain Echo Cap Unstated
**Mechanism:** Cap states "+2 per stat per scene." Negative echoes (stat −1 from Failure) have no stated floor.
**Provisional:** Apply symmetric ±2 cap. ED-NNN if Jordan disagrees.

### P2-03 — PP-482: Baralta Conviction Fires Season 1
**Setup:** PI starts 7. Condition: PI ≥ 6. 7 ≥ 6 = true in Season 1.
**Effect:** Hafenmark Mandate +1 guaranteed in Season 1 Accounting.
**Severity:** P2 — not a dramatic conditional, just a free Mandate point. Redesign needed.
**Proposed fix:** Change to: PI ≥ 6 AND Hafenmark has successfully used Parliamentary Challenge or Parliamentary Session this arc. OR: AND Baralta Mandate ≥ 5 (high-water mark condition).

### P2-04 — PP-490: Schism Breaks Temperance Chain
**Setup:** Church in Season 5/6 Temperance Focus chain. Stability hits 2, Senator action targets Church, schism fires.
**Effect:** Schisming Cardinal ignores Focus. Season 6 Focus declaration invalid. AER chain broken.
**Provisional:** Schism breaks in-progress Focus chains. Intended counter. Add explicit statement.

### P2-05 — PP-494: Diplomat Inward Contested Torben
**Mechanism:** "All factions bid via Diplomat Inward." Diplomat Inward is Hafenmark-only (PP-446). Other factions cannot bid.
**Fix:** Change to: "All factions with Mandate ≥ 3 may bid via Senator Outward, Ob = target's current Torben Loyalty ÷ 2."

### P2-06 — PP-495: Church Prominence Against Uncontrolled Territory
**Mechanism:** Church Seizure requires Prominence (Church Mandate > controlling faction Mandate). Uncontrolled territory has no controlling faction. Prominence undefined.
**Provisional:** Uncontrolled territories: Church can seize without Prominence gate. Ob = 2 + Fort + max(0, 3 − CV).

### P2-07 — J-05: Post-Maret Succession Varfell Has No Victory Path
**Setup:** Maret Uln succession fires. VTM → 0. All Varfell paths require VTM ≥ 3.
**Severity:** P2 — Varfell player has no viable path after succession.
**Proposed fix:** Add post-succession co-victory: if Maret succession active AND RM achieves Cultural Uprising of T9: Varfell+RM automatic co-victory declared.

### P2-08 — D-07: Reconstitution When PI Already ≥ 1
**Mechanism:** Reconstitution defined for "PI = 0 recovery." If PI = 1 somehow, Reconstitution has no effect.
**Fix:** Add scope note: Reconstitution is only available and valid when PI = 0.

### P2-09 — F-493-02: Reconstitution Activates Institutional Mandate Protection
**Mechanism:** Success → Mandate +1 → Mandate 4 → Uphold/Appease threshold.
**Assessment:** Intentional and correct. No change.

### P2-10 — L-05: No Reference Card for Simultaneous Coup + Elimination

## Key Findings

### F-SIM-2-001 — Versatile-Heavy catastrophically weak (P1 BALANCE)
**Avg win rate: 5.6–12.8%** — worst builds in the dataset.

Root cause: Triple penalty stacking. Versatile −1D (pool 7→6 effective offence ≈3), Heavy TN 7 (hardest to hit), Heavy parry TN 8 (easiest to defend against). With 3 offence dice at TN 7, expected successes ≈ 0.9/round. Against parry TN 8 on ~4 dice = 1.2 expected. Versatile-Heavy lands fewer hits than it absorbs.

Versatile + Heavy is not a viable build combination at Str 3, pool 8.

**Patch options:**
- A: Versatile −1D applies to offence only when attacking across range bands, not when both at same range (reduces penalty)  
- B: Remove Versatile −1D penalty entirely; Versatile's cost is purely strategic (opponent can still contest range)
- C: Versatile cannot be Heavy — design constraint, not mechanical patch

---

### F-SIM-2-002 — Heavy weapons underperform at Str 3 (P2 BALANCE)
Long-Heavy/None and Short-Heavy/None: **16% avg win rate** — near-bottom of the table.

Root cause: TN 7 attack vs TN 8 parry produces low hit rates at pool 8 (≈4 offence dice). Expected successes at TN 7: 1.2/roll. Expected parry successes at TN 8: 0.8/roll. Hit probability per round: ~35%. The +3 damage bonus rarely fires. Medium weapons (TN 6 vs TN 7) hit ~50% of the time and perform significantly better overall.

Heavy weapons are high-variance, low-frequency damage dealers. At Str 3 with pool 8, the variance doesn't pay off.

**Note:** Heavy weapons likely perform correctly at Str 4+ (pool 9+, more offence dice). This finding may be stat-bracket specific. Flag for re-test at Str 4 run.

---

### F-SIM-2-003 — Heavy armour −2D penalty is correctly punishing at Str 3 (Design confirmation)
Heavy armour builds cluster in the bottom half of the table unless paired with a strong weapon at correct range. Pool 6 (−2D from base 8) with Heavy armour halves effective offence to ≈3 dice — similar to the Versatile-Heavy double-penalty problem.

**Verdict:** Heavy armour at Str 3 is correctly costly. This is working as intended — Heavy armour should require Str 4 to be effective. No patch needed; flag for documentation.

---

### F-SIM-2-004 — Medium weapon + Medium armour dominant (Design confirmation)
Top 3 builds: Long-Medium/Medium (54.1%), Short-Medium/Medium (52.4%), Vers-Medium/Medium (49.5%).

Medium weapon: TN 6 attack / TN 7 parry — balanced hit rate (~50% offence). +2 damage bonus. Medium armour: DR 2, no pool penalty at Str 3, Stamina 3. This is the "competent knight" build and its dominance looks intentional. No patch needed.

---

### F-SIM-2-005 — Short reach now competitive with manoeuvre modelled (Design confirmation)
Short-Medium/Medium: 52.4% avg win rate — second best overall, beating Long-Medium/Medium (54.1%) closely. Short weapon builds are viable once the Agility contest is modelled. The manoeuvre system is functioning correctly.

Short fighters at Long range: expected rounds to close ≈ 3 (p_close ≈ 0.34/round with Agi 3 vs Agi 3). During this phase neither attacks — symmetric cost. Once closed: Short fighter has reach advantage. The balance holds.

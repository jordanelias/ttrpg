# SIM-X-13: Past-Oriented Pulling — Maret Erases Church Evidence
## Mode: C + B (Interaction: Past-Oriented Pulling × Coherence retention × Temporal Disjunction × RS)
## Mechanics: §5 Past-Oriented Pulling × §5.2 Coherence × §7 RS × §13 NPCs
## Context: Maret Uln (TS 50) attempts to Pull a church record — a signed testimony from a witness who observed a Thread operation — displacing it from the archive. The record is 1 season old. RS: 28 (Stirring; within ≤60 threshold required for Past-Oriented Pulling). Witness (Brother Haelmund, now recovering from X-06 wounds) has a living memory of the event.

## Prerequisites Check

**Past-Oriented Pulling requirements:**
- TS 70+ — **FAIL.** Maret's TS is ~50. Past-Oriented Pulling requires TS 70+.
- RS ≤ 60 — Met (RS 28).
- Diagnosis mandatory — Can be satisfied.

**Finding F-54 [P2]:** Maret cannot perform Past-Oriented Pulling. TS 50 is below the 70+ minimum. This is a hard gate — not a penalty, a disqualification. The scenario must be revised: either Maret does not attempt this, or a TS 70+ practitioner is used.

**Revised scenario:** Maret cannot Pull. She considers Dissolution of the document instead (FR-D-01, Object scale, Ob 4, TS 50+). This is within her capability.

**Alternative sub-test:** Simulate Past-Oriented Pulling with a hypothetical TS 70 practitioner (unnamed scholar, pool: Spirit 5 + History Einhir 3 + TPS÷2 = 5+6+3 = 14D÷2 = 7D) to stress-test the mechanics specifically.

## Part A: Maret — FR-D-01 Dissolution of Evidence

**Operation:** FR-D-01 (Dissolution of Evidence, Object Ob 4, Min TS 50).
**Pool:** Spirit (4) + History Einhir (6) — note: FR uses Spirit + History, no TPS. Pool: 10D TN7.
**Ob:** 4 (Object scale, FR-D-01 base).
**Diagnosis:** Maret Diagnoses the document. The record is 1 season old, firmly actualised (a legal testimony carries institutional weight — threads toward evidence of fact). Ob stays at 4 (no modifier for institutional weight beyond the base).

P(10D TN7 ≥4 net) ≈ 58%.

| Degree | Outcome | RS | P |
|--------|---------|-----|---|
| Overwhelming (≥7) | Dissolves cleanly. RS −3. Micro-Gap closes within scene. | −3 | ~12% |
| Success (4–6) | Dissolves. RS −5. Gap forms, lasts 1 scene, closes. | −5 | ~46% |

| Failure (≤1) | Full Gap tears open. RS −8. Monstrous Incursion. Maret Incapacitated. | −8 | ~14% |

**Most likely: Success (46%).** Document dissolves. RS: 28→23. Gap forms in archive room, lasts 1 scene, closes.

**Coherence retention:** FR operation. Ob 4 (single op in this Leap). Maret pool for retention: Spirit (4) + History (6) + TPS (5) = 15D TN7 vs Ob 4. P(retain) ≈ 88%. Most likely: no Coherence loss.

But: FR Dissolution has a degree-table Coherence cost. On Failure: "Coherence −1 (cap)" — but we got Success. Degree-table cost on Success: none listed. The retention roll already covers it. Coherence: 10 (unchanged).

**Temporal Disjunction:** The document is gone but Haelmund's memory persists. Disjunction fires: Haelmund remembers signing a document that no longer exists. Certainty check: Spirit TN7 Ob1. Haelmund Spirit (estimated 3): 3D. P(≥1 net) ≈ 63%. Most likely: passes. No Certainty loss. But Haelmund knows something is wrong.

**Witness (Klapp, TS 50, present in archive from X-11):** Klapp perceives the Gap forming (TS 50 perceives operation type and approximate target). CE +2: Klapp CE 6→8 (already at ceiling of trajectory determination).

**RS impact:** RS 28→23. Still in Stirring band (20–39). No threshold crossing.

## Part B: Hypothetical TS 70 Scholar — Past-Oriented Pulling on Same Record

**Character:** Unnamed TS 70 practitioner. Spirit 5, History Einhir 3, TPS÷2 = 3 (round down from 7÷2).
**Pool:** Spirit + History + TPS÷2 = 5+6+3 = 14D TN7. Wait — that's wrong. Re-read: "Pool: Spirit + relevant History bonus + TPS÷2 (round down)". So pool = 5+6+(70÷10÷2) = 5+6+3 = 14D. Correct.
**Ob:** Recency 1–2 seasons → Ob 4.
**Diagnosis mandatory:** met.
**RS ≤ 60:** RS 23 (after Part A). Met.

P(14D TN7 ≥4 net) ≈ 88%.

But: Active Knot Crisis modifier. Haelmund has a living memory of the event (the loss still raw — he witnessed the document's disappearance as a Disjunction event). "If the target event has caused an active Knot Crisis in any character... add +1 Ob." Haelmund is not in a Knot Crisis from this (Certainty check passed, no crisis), but the Disjunction is recent and raw. GM call: +1 Ob applies (the event produced a Disjunction within this scene). Ob: 4+1 = 5.

P(14D TN7 ≥5 net) ≈ 72%.

| Degree | Outcome | RS | P |
|--------|---------|-----|---|
| Overwhelming (≥8) | Temporal displacement clean. RS −3 additional. | −3 | ~42% |
| Success (5–7) | Physical facts removed. Haelmund's memory creates Disjunction. RS −3. | −3 | ~30% |
| Partial (3–4) | Partial displacement. Haelmund retains memory AND some physical trace remains. RS −4. Fraying Bane risk (Spirit Ob 1). | −4 | ~20% |
| Failure (≤2) | Snap-back. 1 Wound. RS −3 minimum. Temporal Gap forms. | −3+ | ~8% |

**Most likely: Overwhelming (42%).** Temporal displacement clean. Total RS cost: −3 (Part A Dissolution) −3 (Part B Pull) = RS 28−6 = **22**.

**Coherence retention (Part B):** Past-Oriented Pulling Ob 5 in Leap (this is a separate Leap from Part A — different practitioner, different contact window). Retention pool: 14D TN7 vs Ob 5 (single op).
P(retain, 14D vs Ob5) ≈ 72%. Most likely: retain. Coherence unchanged (hypothetical practitioner starts at 10).

**Additional Coherence note:** Past-Oriented Pulling has "+1 Ob additional on top of standard Pulling cost" in the Coherence reduction table (stage3 L601). Under new retention system: this means the retention roll Ob is Ob (operation) + 1 = 5+1 = 6. Revised P(14D vs Ob6) ≈ 57%. Still likely retain.

**Finding F-55 [P2]:** The "+1 additional" Coherence cost for Past-Oriented Pulling maps cleanly to the retention roll: add +1 to the total Ob sum. This is the correct interpretation under the new system. The prior per-operation wording translates to: Past-Oriented Pulling Ob = stated Ob + 1 for retention purposes.

## MODE B — INTERACTION CHAIN

**B1. Direct Belief opposition (Aldric):** Pre-Leap Belief check Spirit 3D Ob1 → P(pass): 60.8%. Drops out 39% of the time. Pool: 16D (no lattice). Still viable.

**B2. Aldric Leap failure:** Pool 16D Ob3 → P(Suc+): 75.3% (vs 79.1% collective). Lattice contributes +9% success probability — meaningful but bounded. Two independent Weavings → double OA stacking (ruling: second Weaving at +1 Ob from active OA).

**B3. Overwhelming result (P=48.9%):** RS+1, +1 TS (Mira), OA duration halved (brittle window ≈ half-season). Overwhelming Weave is meaningfully safer than Success Weave. Large pools reduce both failure risk AND brittleness exposure.

**B4. Weaving failure (P=8.5%):** RS−2, Coherence−1 each, no stabilisation. No Shifting Object forms at RS 58 (above RS 40 threshold). Political failure, no world damage.

**B5. Lock vs. Weave comparison:**
- Weave: 79% success rate but brittle (non-Thread stress = Shifting Object)
- Lock: 13.4% success rate but immune to brittleness; instead causes RS drift −1/season (dynamic domain) and political incoherence if real-world events violate the locked state
- **FINDING: Lock and Weave are genuine strategic alternatives at Relational scale with complementary failure modes.**
